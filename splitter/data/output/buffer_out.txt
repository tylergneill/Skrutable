umāpatim sarva-jagat-patim sadā praṇamya nirvāṇa-dam īśvaram param
_
gurūn ca sarvān anumokṣa-siddhaye pravakṣyate nyāyasat-artha-saṅgrahaḥ

tasya-ādau apraṇamya-a ityādi vākyam maṅgala-artham sambandha-prayojana-abhidheya-pratipatti-artham ca

praṇāma-kṛtena hi maṅgalena-adharma-pratibandhakena-adharma-mūlāḥ vighna-vināyakāḥ protsāryante

tatas śāstra-parisamāptiḥ iti arthavān praṇāmaḥ

yatra tu na samāptiḥ tatra praṇāmasya asamyak-tvam anumeyam karttuḥ adharma-bāhulyam vā
_
na ca-evam asāmarthyam eva praṇāmasya
_
anyatra sāmarthyāt-
_
na hi pradīpta-hasti-śālā-agni-upaśama-asamarthasya kumbha-śata-udakasya-anyatra-api asāmarthyam dṛṣṭam
_
yatra-api antareṇa praṇāmam śāstra-parisamāptiḥ dṛṣṭā tatra-api mānasaḥ kāyikaḥ vā praṇāmaḥ anumeyaḥ
_
sādhana-antara-sādhyaḥ vā dharmaḥ
_
vācika-praṇāme tu anyeṣām api upadeśa-siddhiḥ syāt-
_
sarvā-api kriyā maheśvara-praṇāma-pūrvikā-eva mumukṣuṇā viśeṣeṇa-eva karttavyā
_
yena śāstra-parisamāpti-vat mokṣa-samāptiḥ api avighnataḥ syāt iti
_
atha vighna-hetuḥ adharmaḥ eva na-asti
_
dharmaḥ vā-asti tad-pratibandhakaḥ pūrva-kṛtaḥ

tathā-api tad-āśaṅkayā karttavyaḥ eva praṇāmaḥ

śatru-ādi-āśaṅkayā-astra-ādi-saṅgraha-vat iti

##
śrotṛ-pravṛtti-artham sambandha-ādi-abhidhānam

na ca-anavasthā

prathamam jijñāsā-ataḥ kāpīḍayā-api pravṛtteḥ sambhavāt-

kim tarhi

tathā ca sūtram
_
pramāṇa-ādi-tattva-jñānāt niḥśreyasa-adhigamaḥ iti

##
atra śāstra-śiṣya-ācāryāṇām nyāya-labhya-tvāt na-abhidhānam kṛtam

na

prabodha-ākhyasya prayojanasya-abhidhānāt eva labdha-tvāt-

tad-artha-tvāt prabodhasya

tad-artha-tvam ca-uttaratra vakṣyāmaḥ

veda-sūtra-śrutitaḥ vā tattva-jñānam niḥśreyasa-hetu-tvena niścitam

samyak adhyātma-jñāna-rahitānām anya-śāstra-upādhyāyānām api atra-upasannānām putra-vat vyutpādya-tva-jñāpana-artham śiśu-grahaṇam

##
dvividham atra-abhidheyam

lakṣaṇam eva pradhānam prāptam

tatpuruṣasya-uttara-pada-prādhānyāt iti ced-
_
na
_
pūrva-pada-prādhānyasya-api dṛṣṭa-tvāt-
_
yathā chātra-śatam bhojitam iti

bhavatu vā lakṣaṇasya prādhānyam tathā ca sūtrakāraḥ pramāṇa-ādīnām tattvam
iti śaiṣikyā ṣaṣṭhyā-artha-antaram tattvam uktvā tattva-jñānāt niḥśreyasa-adhigamaḥ iti āha
_
tattvam ca pramāṇa-ādīnām lakṣaṇam eva-ucyate

pramāṇa-ādīnām avijñeya-tvam avyākhyeya-tvam ca prāptam iti etat api acodyam

na hi go-lakṣaṇe khuraka-kudada-gotva-sāsna-ādi-saṃjñitaḥ gauḥ aniścitaḥ bhavati
_
pratyuta su-niścitaḥ eva bhavati
_
tathā hi
_
sva-niścaya-sāmarthyāt padārthānām paraspara-asaṃkareṇa niścāyakaḥ dharmaḥ lakṣaṇam
iti ucyate tad-aniścaye padārthānām api aniścayaḥ eva-iti atas prādhānyam lakṣaṇasya-iti

##
nanu ca-uktam

prasiddhāni pramāṇāni vyavahāraḥ ca tad-kṛtaḥ

pramāṇa-lakṣaṇasya-uktau jñāyate na prayojanam

iti evam prameya-ādiṣu api vācyam iti

anena-etat ucyate
_

gavā-ādeḥ sāsna-ādi-lakṣaṇena-eka-kāla-tva-darśanāt
_
ātma-ādi-lakṣyasya ca nitya-tvāt-
_
na-api jñāpyate
_
lakṣaṇa-aprasiddhau api loke lakṣya-pratīti-darśanāt-
_
anavasthā-prasaṅgaḥ ca durnivāraḥ syāt-
_
atha pravṛtti-nivṛtti-rūpaḥ
_
śābdaḥ-
_
bādhya-bādhaka-vyavahāraḥ ca pramāṇa-ādiṣu lakṣaṇam antareṇa na sidhyati-iti
_
na
_
tasya-api siddha-tvāt-
_
loke bādhya-bādhaka-lakṣaṇaḥ api siddhaḥ eva-iti
_
saṃjñā niyamena-aprasiddhā-iti ced-
_
na
_
prasiddha-tvāt-
_
tathā hi strī-śūdra-ādayaḥ api bruvantaḥ dṛśyante
_
tvadīyam vacaḥ pratyakṣeṇa-eva bādhyate iti evam ādi
_
tasmāt pramāṇa-ādi-lakṣaṇam niṣprayojanam iti
_

##
atra tāvat utpatti-pakṣe anabhyupagamaḥ eva parihāraḥ
_
pratipattiḥ tu lakṣaṇa-dvāreṇa dṛṣṭā-eva gavā-ādau
_
tathā hi
_
bahuṣu api goṣu asādhāraṇa-lakṣaṇa-darśanena-ātmīyām gām niścinoti
_
lakṣaṇān abhijñaḥ tu sandihyate iti
_
evam sarvatra sthāvara-jaṅgama-ratna-ādi-viśeṣeṣu api udāharaṇam draṣṭavyam
_
lakṣaṇasya-api lakṣaṇa-antareṇa niścaye sati anavasthā-iti ced-
_
na
_
sandeha-viṣayaḥ eva niyama-abhyupagamāt-
_
yatra-eva vastuni sandehaḥ tatra-eva lakṣaṇa-upalambha-pūrvakaḥ niścayaḥ iti ayam niyamaḥ abhyupagamyate
_
na ca sarvatra sandehaḥ asti
_
na hi sthāṇu-ādiṣu iva vakra-ādi-viśeṣeṣu api sandehaḥ utpadyate
_
tad-abhyupagame ca pratyakṣa-ādi-virodhaḥ syāt-
_
atha kasmāt sthāṇu-ādiṣu eva sandehaḥ bhavati
_
na vakra-ādi-viśeṣa-paraṃparāsu api-iti
_
sthāṇu-ādiṣu sandeha-kāraṇa-sadbhāvāt-itaratra-asadbhāvāt iti
_
na
_
atra-api paryanuyoga-anivṛtteḥ
_
kasmāt tad-viśeṣa-paraṃparāsu api sandeha-kāraṇam na bhavati-iti
_
vastu-svabhāvaiḥ atra-uttaram vācyam
_
ye evam bhavanti
_

##
vayam tu yathādṛṣṭam artham bruvāṇāḥ na-upālambham arhāmaḥ
_
sandeha-viṣaye api niścayaḥ viśeṣa-darśanāt-
_
na lakṣaṇa-darśanāt iti ced-
_
na
_
viśeṣa-aṅka-cihna-lakṣaṇa-śabdānām paryāya-tvāt-
_
avyāpakāti-vyāpakayoḥ api lakṣaṇa-tva-prasaṅgaḥ iti ced-
_
na
_
ati-vyāpakasya-api viśeṣaka-tvāt-
_
viṣāṇa-ādi api kenacid vailakṣaṇyena-avagamyate yadā
_
tadā go-lakṣaṇa-tvena-iṣṭam
_
avyāpakaḥ api viśeṣaḥ lakṣaṇa-tvena-abhyupagamyate

yathā dravyasya kriyāvat-tvam
_
na hi kriyāvat-tvam upalabhyamānam dravya-niścayam na karoti

yat eva kriyāvat tat eva dravyam iti abhiprāyavataḥ khalu avyāpti-doṣaḥ udbhāvyate niṣkriyasya-ākāśa-ādeḥ adravya-tva-prasaṅgaḥ iti

##
etena-icchā-ādi-a-ātma-lakṣaṇam

ceṣṭā-āśraya-tvam śarīrasya

yugapad-jñāna-anutpattiḥ manasaḥ
_
vimṛśya-ityādi-nirṇayasya lakṣaṇam ca vyākhyātam
_
lakṣya-antare tu mukta-ātma-ādau ukta-lakṣaṇa-abhāve api lakṣaṇa-antarāt ātma-ādi-vyavahāraḥ sidhyati

yathā kriyāvat-tva-abhāve api ākāśa-ādiṣu guṇavat-tva-ādi-lakṣaṇāt dravya-vyavahāraḥ
_
evam ātma-tvāt mukta-ātmasvātma-vyavahāraḥ
_
raṇḍa-antaḥkaraṇeṣu manaḥ-tvāt manaḥ-vyavahāraḥ
_
śilā-antargata-śarīreṣu śarīra-tvāt indriya-āśraya-tvāt ca śarīra-vyavahāraḥ
_
kara-tala-ādi-nirṇayeṣu avyabhicāri niścaya-anubhava-tvāt nirṇaya-vyavahāraḥ
_
iti evam anyatra-api ūhyam iti
_
lakṣaṇa-antaram eva-astu vyāpaka-tvāt-
_
anarthakam avyāpaka-lakṣaṇa-abhidhānam iti ced-

na

asya-api sva-lakṣya-niścaye samartha-tvāt-

ātma-tva-ādi-pratipattau icchā-ādi-lakṣaṇasya-eva samartha-tvāt ca

##
lakṣaṇe api vipratipattau lakṣaṇa-antara-abhidhāne sati anavasthā-iti ced-

na

sarvatra vipratipatti-asambhavāt-

katham

atha kiṃcid api na pratipadyate
_
tadā puruṣa-viśeṣam prati śabda-viśeṣa-uccāranam na prāpnoti

kutas-
_
vṛkṣa-ādikam dṛṣṭvā-api yat kiṃcid brūyāt-

tatas ca-unmatta-vat upekṣaṇīyaḥ syāt-

##
na ca-iyam lakṣaṇa-paraṃpara-abhyupagamyate

kasmāt-

lakṣaṇa-lakṣaṇasya sakala-lakṣaṇa-varga-vyavacchedaka-tvena sva-ātmanaḥ api vyavacchedaka-tvāt-

yathā anityaḥ sarvaḥ śabdaḥ iti etat-vākyam sva-ātmanaḥ api anitya-tām abhidhatte

svādhyāyaḥ adhyetavyaḥ iti ayam vidhiḥ sva-ātmanaḥ api adhyeya-tvam vidhattaḥ iti

karma-karaṇayoḥ eka-kriyāyām eka-tvam ayuktam iti ced-
_
na
_
pramātṛ-prameya-vat kvacid dṛṣṭa-tvāt-

tat-dṛṣṭāntena-eva sarvadā-api abheda-prasaṅgaḥ iti ced-

na

paṭaturī-vema-ādīnām bhedasya pratyakṣa-ādi-siddha-tvāt

tat evam sarvatra sandeha-viṣaye niścaya-hetu-tvāt na lakṣaṇa-vacanam niṣprayojanam iti

##
lakṣaṇasya-eva niścaya-hetu-tve nirarthakam pramāṇam iti ced-

na

lakṣaṇasya-api niścaya-sādhya-tvena pramāṇa-tvāt-

asmābhiḥ tu abhihitam eva viśeṣa-aṅga-ādi-śabdāḥ paryāyāḥ iti

tasya-avayava-ādi-vat prayojana-vaśāt pṛthak abhidhānam

##
śāstram antareṇa-api pramāṇa-ādi-lakṣaṇam prasiddham eva

anyathā laukikā katham pramāṇa-ādi-vyavahāram kurvanti-iti atas anarthakam tad-artham śāstram iti ced-

na
_
bhrānti-nivṛttaye anuvāda-karaṇāt-

darśana-antara-abhiniviṣṭaiḥ khalu bhrāntaiḥ anyathā-anyathā lakṣaṇa-abhidhānena loke api pramāṇa-ādiṣu visaṃvādyate

tathā hi
_
pratyakṣa-ādi-lakṣaṇam anūdya yatra bhrāntāḥ samyak pramāṇeṣu api ātma-ādi-viṣayeṣu pramāṇa-abhāsa-tām
anartha-antareṣu api arthāpatti-ādiṣu artha-antara-tām ca pratipadyante

tatra vidhīyate

yatra punar pramāṇa-ābhāseṣu api advaita-kṣaṇika-tva-vedāḥ nitya-tva-ātma-ādya-sattva-ādi-viṣayeṣu pratyakṣa-ādi-pramāṇa-tām

tatra pratiṣidhyate

##
etena-ātma-ādi-jñānam upāya-tvena niḥśreyasam ca-upeya-tvena-upaniṣatsu anyeṣu ca yoga-śāstreṣu prasiddham

na
_
hi atra-ātma-ādi-vijñānam niḥśreyasam vā vidhīyate kim tarhi

ātma-ādi-samarthana-dvāreṇa-aśeṣa-pratipakṣa-pratiṣedha-dvāreṇa ca pramāṇa-upapanna-tvam tasya vidhīyate

eva-upāyam anutiṣṭhataḥ apavarga-prāptiḥ bhavati-iti

##
tathā ca-ātmā-ādīnām pramāṇa-upapanna-tvam pratipādya adhyātma-vidhi-upāyaiḥ iti uktam śrīmat-sūtra-kṛtā-iti

nyāya-kāṅkṣiṇaḥ sārthaka-tvāt-

sarva-puruṣa-anugrahasya-aśakya-tvāt

ānantyāt avyavasthita-abhiprāya-tvāt ca prāṇinām

adhikāriṇaḥ ca-atra santi eva nyāya-kāṃkṣiṇaḥ sandigdha-ādi-viśeṣāḥ

yeṣām api ukti-mātrāt adhyātma-śāstra-artha-niścaye sati vipratipattim śṛṇvatām api kutaścid nimittāt sandehaḥ na bhavati

teṣām api-idam śāstram śrotum yuktam para-anugraha-artham sva-niścaya-pālana-artham ca
_
tathā ca-uktam

tattva-adhyavasāya-saṃrakṣaṇa-artham ityādi

evam ca sarvāṇi uddeśa-lakṣaṇa-parāṇi sūtrāṇi anuvāda-arthāni

parīkṣā-sūtrāṇi tu anuvāda-artha-phalena vidhi-pratiṣedha-saṃjñakena-arthavanti draṣṭavyāni-iti evam para-tā-asya śāstrasya-iti

##
nanu ca kṛṣi-ādiṣu śāstra-śravaṇe ca yadvat-artha-saṃśayāt eva-arthinām

na

viparyaḥ tu apavarga-sādhane atas anartha-saṃśaya-nivṛtti-hetuḥ sa-sādhanaḥ apavarga-niścayaḥ prārthyate prekṣā-pūrva-kāribhiḥ

samyak-anubhava-sādhanam pramāṇam iti

anena samāna-jātīyebhyaḥ pramāṇa-ābhāsebhyaḥ asamāna-jātīyebhyaḥ ca prameya-ādibhyaḥ vyavacchedaḥ prasādhyate

##
pramāṇasya samāna-jātīyāni vyavacchedyāni na-eva santi-iti yaḥ manyate tam pratyāha
_
samyak-grahaṇam saṃśaya-viparyaya-apoha-artham
_
saṃśaya-viparyayayoḥ pramāṇa-phala-tvena vyāvṛttiḥ nirākaraṇam apohaḥ tad-artham
_
atha vā saṃśaya-viparyayābhyām sakāśāt apohaḥ vyāvṛttiḥ anubhavasya tad-artham iti arthaḥ
_
tatas tad-sādhanānām api pramāṇa-tvam vyavacchinnam bhavati
_
phalasya hi samyaktva-asamyaktvābhyām pramāṇa-tad-ābhāsayoḥ samyaktva-asamyaktve niścetum śakyete
_
tena samyak iti etat phala-viśeṣaṇa-tvena vyākhyāyate
_
samyak ca-asau anubhavaḥ ca-iti
_
tathābhūta-artha-niścaya-svabhāva-tvam samyak-tvam
_
tad-viparīta-anubhava-svabhāva-tvam asamyak-tvam
_

##
tat ca saṃśaya-viparyayayoḥ asti loka-śāstrayoḥ tathā-avyavahāra-darśanāt iti
_
na-ajñāta-svarūpam hātum upādātum vā śakyam iti atas tayoḥ svarūpam vaktavyam iti
_
atha-idam ucyate
_
tatra-anavadhāraṇa-jñānam saṃśayaḥ
_
anavadhāraṇam ca tat-jñānam ca-iti
_
vyāghātāt na yuktam iti ced-
_
na
_
go-śabda-ādi-vat jāti-nimitta-tvāt jñāna-śabdasya
_
yathā gopaṅkaja-ādi-śabdāḥ na vyutpatti-vaśena sva-artheṣu rūḍhāḥ
_
kim tarhi
_
jāti-nimitta-tvena tathā jñāna-śabdaḥ api jñāna-tva-jāti-nimittaḥ sva-arthe nirūḍhaḥ
_
sā ca jñāna-tva-ādi-jātiḥ niścaya-aniścaya-svabhāvāsu vyaktiṣu vartate
_
aniścaya-jñānam ca-anavadhāraṇam iti uktam
_
atas eva nirvikalpaka-pratyakṣasya saṃśaya-tvam na prasajyate
_
tasya-api niścaya-ātmaka-tvāt-
_
niścaya-ātmaka-tvam ca tad-prabhava-smṛti-anumeyam
_
tathā hi
_
idam vastu tatra na-asti eva
_
anena sadṛśam eva tat-vastu
_
etasmāt adhikam nyūnam ca-iti evam ātmikā smṛtiḥ pūrva-anubhavam niścaya-ātmakam sūcayati
_

##
yatas ca-anubhavāt aniścaya-ātmikā smṛtiḥ utpadyate sa saṃśayaḥ eva-aniścaya-ātmaka-tvāt
_
anubhava-ākāra-anukāriṇī hi smṛtiḥ
_
sā katham anyathā bhavet iti
_
prathama-akṣa-darśana-jam jñānam viśeṣa-darśana-ādi-nimittam antareṇa-upajāyamānam
niścaya-ātmakam saṃśaya-ātmakam vā katham bhavitum arhati-iti ced-
_
na
_
adṛṣṭa-ādi-sāmagrī-vaśāt tathā-utpatteḥ
_
anyathā-anavasthā-bhayāt viśeṣa-darśanam antareṇa-upajātam viśeṣa-darśanam aniścaya-ātmakam syāt-
_
tasya ca-aniścaya-ātmaka-tve viśeṣavataḥ api aniśa-cayaḥ iti aniścaya-ātmakam jagat syāt-
_
na hi śiraḥ-pāṇi-ādi-viśeṣa-aniścaya-jñānāt puruṣe aniścayaḥ dṛṣṭaḥ
_
adṛṣṭa-ādeḥ ca sāmarthya-abhyupagame tatas eva niścayaḥ bhavatu
_
kim aniścaya-ātmakena viśeṣa-darśanena-anupapanna-pramāṇakena-iti
_
tat evam viśeṣa-darśana-ādi-nimittam antareṇa-api kvacid niścaya-ātmakam aniścaya-ātmakam ca jñānam asti-iti atas niravadyam lakṣaṇam
_
anavadhāraṇa-jñānam saṃśayaḥ iti
_

##
yadi etau atā-eva saṃśaya-lakṣaṇam niravadyam samāna-aneka-dharma-upapatteḥ ityādi-sūtram tarhi anarthakam
_
na-anarthakam vibhāga-artham viśeṣa-lakṣaṇa-artham ca
_
tad-artham eva-āha
_
sa ca-ityādi
_
pañcadhā bhidyate samāna-dharma-ādi-mātrāt asambhavī saṃśayaḥ iti ced-
_
na
_
anavadhāraṇāt-
_
na hi evam avadhāryate samāna-dharmāt eva saṃśayaḥ iti
_
kim tarhi
_
indriya-artha-sannikarṣa-vat asādhāraṇa-kāraṇa-vacanam samāna-jātīya-vyavaccheda-artham
_
vijātīya-vyavacchedaḥ tu sāmānya-lakṣaṇena-eva kṛta-tvāt na-anukriyate
_
sāmānya-lakṣaṇa-anuvādena hi viśeṣa-lakṣaṇam draṣṭavyam
_
tena-ayam arthaḥ siddhyati
_
samāna-dharmāt sāmagrī-antar-patitāt anavadhāraṇa-jñānam bhavati
_
tad-aneka-dharma-ādi-upalakṣita-sāmagrī-janitebhyaḥ saṃśayebhyaḥ artha-antaram iti
_
evam anyatra-api vyākhyātavyam iti atas na-ati-vyāpti-doṣaḥ api asti
_

##
sūtrakāreṇa tu-upalabdhi-anupalabdhi-avyavasthā-ādi-grahaṇam ku-codya-parihāra-artham sahakāryu-dāha-rana-artham ca kṛtam
_
na tu anena-eva saṃpūrṇā sāmagrī pratipādyate
_
adṛṣṭa-antaḥkaraṇa-ādeḥ anabhidhānāt-
_
kuca-udyam punar atra-evam bhavati
_
samāna-dharma-upalambhaḥ api na saṃśaya-kāraṇam
_
sati api tasmin gacchataḥ tṛṇa-ādiṣu saṃśaya-anupalabdheḥ iti
_
tatra-uttaram
_
viśeṣa-apekṣā iti viśeṣeṣu apekṣā ākāṅkṣā smṛti-rūpā yasya saṃśayasya kāraṇa-tvena sa tathā-uktaḥ
_
kim uktam bhavati
_
viśeṣa-ākāṅkṣā ca yatas saṃśaya-kāraṇam
_
atas tām antareṇa-api saṃśayaḥ na bhavati
_
na ca yasya sadbhāve api sahakāri-kāraṇa-abhāvāt kāryam na-utpadyate tasya-akāraṇa-tvam eva
_
sarvasya-api akāraṇa-tva-prasaṅgāt iti
_

##
nanu evam api dūra-sthāyām priyatamāyām ubhaya-sadbhāve api saṃśayaḥ na bhavati
_
atas akāraṇam viśeṣa-ākāṅkṣā-sahitaḥ api samānaḥ dharmaḥ iti
_
asya-uttaram
_
upalabdhi-anupalabdhi-avyavasthātaḥ iti
_
vidhi-pratiṣedha-tvena niścaya-kāraṇa-abhāvāt iti arthaḥ
_
etat uktam bhavati
_
na viśeṣa-ākāṅkṣā-eva sahakāri-kāraṇam kintu-upalabdhi-anupalabdhi-avyavasthāpi-iti atas tad-abhāve api saṃśayaḥ na bhavati
_
tathā vegena gacchataḥ panasa-ādiṣu vṛkṣa-samāna-dharma-upalambhe api upalabdhi-anupalabdhi-avyavasthāyām
api viśeṣa-ākāṅkṣā-bhāvāt na saṃśayaḥ bhavati-iti atas sakala-sahakārī-upetasya-eva samāna-dharmasya saṃśaya-hetu-tvam abhyupagamyate iti
_
adṛṣṭa-ādeḥ ca kārya-antareṣu api sādhāraṇa-tvāt anabhidhāne api sahakāri-tvam labhyate iti abhiprāyavatā-atra na-uktam
_

##
samāna-dharma-vat aneka-dharma-ādeḥ api aśeṣa-sahakārī-upetasya saṃśaya-hetu-tvam vyākhyeyam iti
_
asya-eva-arthaḥ parīkṣāyām prapañcitaḥ iti apaunaruktyam
_
samāna-dharmaḥ iti atra samāsa-dvayam vikalpena draṣṭavyam
_
karmadhārayaḥ ṣaṣṭhī-tatpuruṣaḥ ca-iti
_
yadā karmadhārayaḥ tadā samānaḥ sadṛśaḥ parimāṇa-ādi-lakṣaṇaḥ dharmaḥ prati āśrayam bhinnaḥ labhyate
_
yadā tatpuruṣaḥ tadā prati āśrayam abhinnaḥ dharmaḥ sāmānya-avayava-saṃyoga-ādi-lakṣaṇaḥ labhyate iti
_
atra-udāharaṇam
_
tat yathā iti
_
ūrdhva-tvam ca diś-viśeṣa-avasthite dīrgha-dravyaḥ ūrdhva-pratyaya-nimittam
_
tat ca parimāṇam vā bhavatu sāmānyam vā ṣaṭpadārtha-vyatirikta-dharma-saṃjñakam vā na-asya vicāraḥ kriyate anupayogi-tvāt iti
_

##
aneka-dharmāt iti
_
vivakṣita-jātīyam tad-viparītam ca-anekam
_
anekasmāt vyāvarttakaḥ dharmaḥ anekasya-asambandhī vā-anekasmin pratiṣiddhaḥ vā-aneka-pratyaya-hetuḥ-
vā bheda-sādhaka-tvena-iti atas aneka-dharmaḥ iti sarvatra madhyama-pada-lopena samāsaḥ
_
sarvathā-api asādhāraṇa-dharmaḥ abhipretaḥ
_
tathā ca-ākāśa-ādi-viśeṣa-guṇa-tvāt kim ayam śabdaḥ nityaḥ syāt anityaḥ vā-iti udāharaṇam
_
katham punar asādhāraṇaḥ dharmaḥ saṃśayam karoti
_
na hi ayam viruddha-viśeṣaiḥ saha kadācid upalabdhaḥ api yena-asya-upalambhaḥ tad-smṛti-hetuḥ syāt-

##
na ca-apratibaddha-artha-darśane smṛtiḥ yuktā
_
sarvadā sarva-anubhūta-artha-smṛti-prasaṅgāt-
_
na ca viruddha-viśeṣa-anusmṛtim antareṇa saṃśayaḥ bhavati
_
adṛṣṭa-tvāt-
_
api ca-ayam asādhāraṇaḥ dharmaḥ sva-dharmiṇām viśeṣaḥ eva
_
viśeṣa-darśanāt ca-anyaḥ api saṃśayaḥ nivarttate
_
katham tatas saṃśayaḥ bhavati-iti
_
na hi go-tva-darśanāt gavi saṃśayaḥ prādurbhavan upalabhyate
_
anuccheda-prasaṅgāt ca
_
yaḥ tāvat samāna-dharma-darśana-utpannaḥ saṃśayaḥ sa viśeṣa-darśanāt nivarttate
_
yaḥ tu viśeṣa-darśanāt utpannaḥ
_
sa kutas pradhvaṃsam āsādayiṣyati-iti
_
tasmāt nāsādhāraṇaḥ dharmaḥ saṃśaya-hetuḥ iti

##
atra-eke pratisamādhānam kurvanti
_
tulya-jātīyeṣu artha-antara-bhūteṣu ca viśeṣasya-ubhayathā dṛṣṭa-tvāt na viruddha-viśeṣaiḥ saha-adarśanam asādhāraṇasya-iti
_
etat uktam bhavati
_
yadā tulya-jātīya-tvena-anityāḥ padārthāḥ vivakṣitāḥ tadā-artha-antara-bhūtāḥ nityāḥ uktāḥ teṣu tulya-jātīyeṣu ghaṭa-ādiṣu artha-antara-bhūteṣu
ca-ātma-ādiṣu viśeṣasya ghaṭa-tva-ādeḥ ātma-tva-ādeḥ ca ubhayathā prakāra-dvayena nitya-tva-anitya-tva-sahacarita-tvena dṛṣṭa-tvāt-
pramāṇena-upalabdha-tvāt iti arthaḥ
_
asti ca śabde viśeṣaḥ śrāvaṇa-tvam ākāśa-viśeṣa-guṇa-tvam ca
_
tat na vidmaḥ
_
kim nityasya sataḥ śabdasya-ayam viśeṣaḥ
_
uta-anityasya-iti
_
na-idam saṃśaya-kāraṇam samāna-dharmāt artha-antaram
_
katham
_
viśeṣavat-tvam hi viruddha-viśeṣa-smṛti-hetu-tvena saṃśaya-nimittam
_
tat ca sāmānyavat-tva-ādi-vat nitya-anityayoḥ samānam iti katham artha-antaram bhavet-
_
pūrvam sādhāraṇa-dharmavat-tvam uktam idānīm tu asādhāraṇa-darmavat-tvam iti etau atā-eva viśeṣeṇa pṛthak-vacanam iti eke

##
vayan tu asādhāraṇena-eva rūpeṇa-asya saṃśaya-hetu-tvam varṇayāmaḥ
_
katham iti
_
nityebhyaḥ vyāvṛtta-tvam
_
anityeṣu eva dṛṣṭam anityebhyaḥ vyāvṛtta-tvam ca nityeṣu eva dṛṣṭam
_
ayan tu śabdaḥ śrāvaṇa-tva-ādi-viśeṣeṇa nityebhyaḥ anityebhyaḥ ca vyāvṛttaḥ
_
tat na jānīmahe kim nityebhyaḥ vyāvṛtta-tvāt ghaṭa-ādi-vat anityaḥ syāt uta-anityebhyaḥ vyāvṛtta-tvāt ātma-ādi-vat nityaḥ iti
_
na ca nitya-tva-anitya-tvayoḥ ekatra sambhavaḥ asti paraspara-parihāra-sthiti-dharma-tvāt
_
na ca-atyanta-saṃśaya-prasaṅgaḥ
_
sahakāri-kāraṇasya-upalabdhi-anupalabdhi-avyavasthā-ādeḥ sātatyena-asambhavāt-
_
na-api anivṛtti-prasaṅgaḥ
_
kṛtaka-tva-ādi-viśeṣa-darśanāt nivṛtteḥ
_
na ca viśeṣa-tva-sāmyāt śrāvaṇa-tva-ādi-vat kṛtaka-tva-āditas api saṃśaya-prasaṅgaḥ
_
kṛtaka-tva-ādeḥ anitya-tva-avinā-abhāvi-tvena-anyatra-upalabdha-tvāt iti
_
tasmāt asādhāraṇaḥ api dharmaḥ saṃśaya-hetuḥ iti sthitam

##
atha vā-anekaḥ ca-asau dharmaḥ ca-iti karmadhārayaḥ tena viruddha-aneka-dharmasya-ekatra-upalabhyamānasya saṃśaya-hetu-tvam labhyate

yathā kim nu kriyāvat-tvāt śara-vat mūrtam manaḥ syāt uta-asparśa-tvāt ākāśa-vat amūrttam iti

paspara-parihāra-sthiti-dharma-tvāt

upalabdhi-anupalabdhi-avyavasthā-ādeḥ ca-atra-api sahakāri-tvam atas na sarvadā sarvatra vā saṃśaya-prasaṅgaḥ iti

tulya-bala-tve tayoḥ virodhāt nirṇaya-anutpādaka-tvam syāt na saṃśaya-utpādaka-tvam iti ced-

na

atas eva tad-siddheḥ

na hi ayam nirṇaya-anutpattau acetana-kuḍya-ādi-vat āste viparyeti vā

paryālocayan praśnam vā kurvan upalabhyate na ca saṃśayam antareṇa paryālocanam praśna-karaṇam vā yujyate

anadhyavasāyaḥ iti ced-

##
atha manyase nirṇaya-anutpattau anadhyavasāyaḥ asya na tu saṃśayaḥ iti

tat ayuktam

vipratipattitaḥ api anadhyavasāya-prasaṅgāt-

anugata-pratyaya-hetu-tvam utkṣepaṇa-ādi-vat pṛthak-abhihita-tvam ca dharma-dvayam saṃśaya-hetu-tvena-uktam iti

tasmāt anekaḥ api dharmaḥ saṃśaya-hetuḥ iti

##
vipratipattiḥ iti
_
vyāhataḥ pravādaḥ vipratipattiḥ
_
tām śṛṇvataḥ pūrva-ukta-sahakāri-sadbhāve sati saṃśayaḥ bhavati
_
kim bhautikāni-indriyāṇi uta-abhautikāni-iti
_
kim śarīra-ādi-vyatiriktaḥ ātmā-asti uta na-asti
_
nityaḥ anityaḥ vā-ityādi-vipratipattayaḥ draṣṭavyāḥ iti
_
vidhi-mukhena pravṛttam jñānam upalabdhiḥ
_
pratiṣedha-mukhena pravṛttam jñānam jñāna-anutpādaḥ ca-anupalabdhiḥ

tayoḥ pṛthak saṃśaya-hetu-tvam avyavasthā-viśeṣaṇa-tvam ca

tena-atra pañca-sūtrī draṣṭvyā

upalabdhi-upapatteḥ upalabdhi-anupalabdhi-avyavasthātaḥ viśeṣa-apekṣaḥ vimarśaḥ saṃśayaḥ

anupalabdhi-upapatteḥ upalabdhi-anupalabdhi-avyavasthātaḥ viśeṣa-apekṣaḥ vimarśaḥ saṃśayaḥ iti

evam samāna-dharma-upapatteḥ ityādiṣu api yojyam

##
bhavatu nāma-evam tathā-api anayoḥ samāna-dharmāt artha-antara-tvam na-asti

katham

yadi tāvat upalabdhi-anupalabdhi-viṣayaḥ eva satya-asatya-tvena saṃśayaḥ tadā-upalabdhi-tvam anupalabdhi-tvam ca sāmānyam

tathā-api jñāna-avabhāsi-tvam anavabhāsi-tvam ca sāmānyam iti
_
atra-eke pariharanti
_
samānaḥ anekaḥ ca dharmaḥ jñeya-sthaḥ
_
upalabdhi-anupalabdhī punar jñātṛ-sthe
_
etāvatā viśeṣeṇa pṛthak-vacanam iti
_
vayam tu brūmaḥ
_
prayojana-vaśāt prthak-abhidhānam
_
katham
_
anupalabdhi-mātreṇa vādinaḥ svarga-apūrva-devatā-ādeḥ asattvam icchanti
_
upalabdhi-mātreṇa śabde sthāyi-tvam śarīre caitanya-ādikam sāmānya-ādeḥ sva-āśrayeṇa-abhedam ca-icchanti

tad-nirākaraṇa-artham upalabdhi-anupalabdhi-mātrasya saṃdeha-nimitta-tvam uktam iti

##
samyak-grahaṇena saṃśaya-viparyayau nirākṛtau

iti yaḥ ca-udayiṣyate tasya-idam uttaram vaktavyam
_
anavadhāraṇa-tva-iti

nirākṛtam iti arthaḥ

tathā ca sūtrakāreṇa saṃśaya-vyavaccheda-artham vyavasāya-ātmaka-grahaṇam eva kṛtam noha-anadhyavasāya-vyavaccheda-artham pada-antaram iti

vikalpena-ubhaya-artha-avalambī saṃśayaḥ tad-vilakṣaṇau tūha-anadhyavasāyau
_
tat katham tayoḥ saṃśayāt na-artha-antara-bhāvaḥ iti ced-
_
na
_
tad-eka-lakṣaṇa-saṅgṛhītayoḥ avāntara-vaidharmya-mātrāt tad-artha-antara-tva-ayogāt
_

##

mayā-idam suvarṇam rajatam vā labdham krītam vā-iti

tasmāt sthāṇu-ādiḥ eva tattvasya-agrahaṇāt adharma-ādi-vaśāt ca puruṣa-ādi-ākāreṇa-adhyavasīyate iti yuktam
_

##
atha svapna-ādi-jñāne kaḥ tarhi viparītaḥ arthaḥ iti
_
tad-deśa-sthaḥ khaṭvādiḥ iti eke
_
tat na
_
pramāṇa-abhāvāt-
_
na hi sannihita-tvāt eva jñānasya viṣayaḥ
_
kim tarhi
_
yaḥ pratibhāti sa ca-uttara-kālam pratisaṃdhāna-balena-api vyavasthāpyate
_
na ca khaṭvādau pratisaṃdhānam api asti
_
tasmāt na-asau viṣayaḥ
_
kim tarhi
_
pūrva-upalabdhaḥ anupalabdhaḥ vā vidyamānaḥ avidyamānaḥ vā yaḥ pratibuddha-avasthāyām bādhaka-pratyayena sthāṇu-ādi-vat anusaṃdhīyate
_
sa khalu deśa-kāla-svabhāva-anya-tvena svapna-jñāne pratibhāsanāt viparītaḥ iti ucyate
_
tathā hi
_
prabuddhaḥ san kathayati
_
mayā-adya svapne mṛtaḥ pitā jīvati-iti dṛṣṭaḥ
_
putraḥ tu deśa-antara-sthitaḥ iha-sthitaḥ iti
_
andhaḥ na-andhaḥ iti evamādi
_

##
keśoṇḍuka-jñāne api avidyamānaḥ keśa-samūhaḥ sat-ākāratayā pratibhāti
_
bādhaka-utpattau tathā-anusaṃdhānāt-
_
nanu ca-avidyamānasya-ālambana-tva-ayogāt nirviṣayaḥ viparyayaḥ prāptaḥ iti
_
na
_
atīta-anāgatayoḥ avidyamāna-tve api ālambana-tvāt-
_
nirālambana-tve hi akhyātiḥ eva syāt-
_
tatra ca doṣaḥ uktaḥ iti
_
atīta-anāgata-svapna-ādi-jñānānām avidyamāna-artha-ālambanatva-aviśeṣāt
_
bhrānta-abhra-anta-viśeṣa-anupapattiḥ iti ced-
_
na
_
tad-rūpā-tad-rūpa-gṛhīta-tvena tattva-jñāna-itarayoḥ viśeṣa-utpatteḥ
_
na hi vidyamānā-vidyamāna-artha-ālambana-tvena tattva-jñāna-itara-vibhāgaḥ
_
kim tarhi
_
avidyamānaḥ api arthaḥ yadā tathābhūtena-eva-avidyamāna-ākāreṇa niścīyate
_
tadā tad-viṣayam tattva-jñānam eva
_
tad-rūpa-avyabhicārāt
_
yadā tu vidyamānena-anyena vā viparīta-ākāreṇa-āsan arthaḥ adhyavasīyate
_
tadā bhrāntiḥ iti
_

##
etena yat āhuḥ eke
_
satya-udakam asatya-udakam ca-iti udaka-prapañcaḥ ayam madhura-udaka-ādi-vat-
_
tatra yathā satya-udaka-buddhau nāsatya-udakam pratibhāti
_
anudakam vā
_
evam asatya-udaka-jñāne api na satya-udakam pratibhāti anudakam vā-iti
_
sva-viṣaye paryavasāyinyaḥ hi buddhayaḥ
_
tasmāt na viparītaḥ asti-iti
_
tat api nirastam
_
na hi asatya-udakam nāma kiṃcid asti
_
vidyamānam api yadi-idam asatya-udakam iti evam niścīyate
_
tadā bhavet abhrānta-jñānam
_
yadā tu pāna-ādi-samartha-udaka-ākāreṇa-adhyavasīyate
_
tadā katham abhrānta-jñānam
_
viparīta-artha-adhyavasāyasya sarva-loka-śāstreṣu bhrānta-tvena prasiddha-tvāt-
_
tad-virodhena ca pravarttamānaḥ ayam na laukikaḥ na parīkṣakaḥ iti upekṣaṇīyaḥ eva-iti
_

##
nanu ca jñānasya bhrānta-tvam kutas avagamyate
_
na pratyakṣāt-
_
tad-artha-lipsayā hi bhrāntasya jñātuḥ pravṛttiḥ na syāt-
_
na-api bādha-utpatti-kāle tasya pratyakṣa-tvam
_
tadā bhrānta-jñānasya-atīta-tvāt-
_
na ca-atītam asmad-ādi-pratyakṣeṇa saṃvedyate iti tena ca-atyanta-apratyakṣeṇa
bādha-utpatteḥ anyasya vā liṅgasya vyāptyaprasiddheḥ anumānam
api na pravarttate
_
na hi sāmānyatas api kārya-kartṛ-ādi-vat atra vyāptiḥ kvacid gṛhītā
_
sarvatra ca-anumānena vyāpti-grahaṇe tu anavasthā syāt iti
_
etena-abhrānta-tvasya-api grahaṇam pratyuktam
_

##
api ca-abhrānta-tvasya liṅgam kim aduṣṭa-kāraka-utpādya-tvam uta bādhā-anutpādaḥ
_
kim vā pravṛtti-sāmarthyam iti
_
na tāvat aduṣṭa-kāraka-utpādya-tvam
_
nayana-adṛṣṭa-ādi-kāraṇānām atīndriya-tvena-aduṣṭa-tvasya-aniścayāt-
_
na hi svayam asiddham liṅgam liṅginaḥ pratipādakam
_
na ca tad-udbhūta-viśiṣṭa-jñānāt eva kārakāt-uṣṭa-tva-siddhiḥ
_
itaretara-āśraya-tva-doṣeṇa-ubhayoḥ asiddheḥ iti
_
na-api bādhā-anutpādaḥ tasya liṅgam
_
bādhā-anutpādasya puruṣa-apravṛtti-deśa-antara-gamana-maraṇa-ādi-nimittāt api sambhavāt-
_
api ca sarva-puruṣa-bādha-anutpatteḥ asarva-vidāvagantum aśakya-tvāt-
_

##
pratipattṛ-bādha-anutpattiḥ tu anaikāntikī katipaya-puruṣāṇām api aneka-kāla-bhrānta-tva-darśanāt
_
kadācid api bādhā na-utpadyate iti etat na-asarva-vidā-avagantum pāryate
_
tasmāt na bādhā-anutpattiḥ api liṅgam
_
na-api pravṛtti-sāmarthyam
_
pravṛtti-sāmarthyam hi artha-prāpti-anartha-parihāra-lakṣaṇam
_
tat ced anavagatam katham asiddham gamakam bhavet-
_
atha-avagatam
_
tad-avagateḥ avyabhicāri-tvam katham avagamyate iti aparihāryā pūrva-ukta-vikalpa-paramparā syāt iti
_
api ca candra-ādi-pratibhāsasya-abhrānta-tvam na sidhyet-
_
candra-ādi-arthasya prāpti-asambhavāt
_
na ca-udaka-arthasya-api pratibhātasya prāptiḥ sambhavati
_
tasya vāyu-ādibhiḥ prāṇi-viśeṣaiḥ ca-avayava-kriyā-utpādana-dvāreṇa pratikṣaṇam vināśyamāna-tvāt-
_
tad-jātīya-artha-prāpteḥ tu anaikāntika-tvāt na gamaka-tvam
_
tad-vaṃśa-ja-artha-prāptiḥ gamikā-iti ced-
_
na
_
antyāvayavinām ajanaka-tvena tad-vaṃśa-ja-artha-asambhavāt-
_
na ca tad-asiddhau tad-vaṃśa-jaḥ ayam arthaḥ iti anumānam kiṃcid asti
_
tat evamādibhiḥ dūṣaṇaiḥ avyabhicāri-tvasya liṅgam nirākṛtam iti atas na liṅgataḥ api grahaṇam iti
_

##
atra-ucyate
_
yadi bhrānta-tva-ādi-niścayaḥ na-asti
_
katham tarhi ayam loka-śāstreṣu vyavahāraḥ
_
samyak avagatam mayā
_
mithyā jñātam tvayā-iti
_
na hi vastu-tathā-tva-niścayam antareṇa tad-vyavahāraḥ yujyate
_
mithyā-niścaya-pūrvakaḥ ayam vyavahāraḥ iti ced-
_
nanu niścayasya mithātvān-avagame katham jānīṣe mithyā-niścaya-pūrvakaḥ vyavahāraḥ iti
_
yaḥ ca-atra pratipatti-upāyaḥ tvayā-ucyate
_
tatas eva-anyatra-api bhrānta-tva-niścayaḥ tad-viparyayāt ca-abhrānta-tva-niścayaḥ api-iti katham sarvatra bhrānta-vādi-niścaya-abhāvaḥ iti
_

##
na mayā pramāṇam kvacid ucyate sarvatra sandeha-mātra-pradarśanāt iti ced-
_
nanu sandehaḥ api ubhaya-adarśane sati bhavati
_
tatas ca sthāṇu-tva-puruṣa-tva-ādi-vat bhrānta-tva-ādi-niścayaḥ kvacid abhyupagantavyaḥ
_
tad-siddhau ca tad-nimittam pramāṇam yat upapadyate tat avaśyam tvayā-api cintanīyam
_
prasiddha-vyavahāra-nimitta-cintā-paratvāt parīkṣakāṇām
_
sandeha-pradarśanam api na-apramāṇena yujyate
_
yena-eva pradarśyate tasya-eva pramāṇa-tvāt-
_
katham na pramāṇam kvacid ucyate
_
atha-aneka-vikalpaiḥ parasya sandehaḥ kriyate na tu pradarśyate iti
_
na
_
aneka-vikalpānām sandeha-janakatvā-niścaye sati upādāna-ayogāt
_

##
na hi kāraṇasya sāmarthya-aniścaye kārya-viśeṣam pratyupādānam yuktam
_
yasya kasyacid upādāna-prasaṅgāt-
_
sandehe api tantu-ādīnām kārya-viśeṣam pratyupādānam dṛṣṭam iti ced na
_
tantu-ādīnām api paṭa-ādi-kārya-utpādana-yogya-tāyāḥ niścita-tvāt-
_
tantu-ādi-abhāve paṭa-ādi-kārya-anutpādasya ca niścita-tvāt niyata-upādānam yuktam
_
bhavataḥ tu na kvacid niścayaḥ iti apravṛttiḥ avyavasthita-pravṛttiḥ vā prasaktā-iti
_
tasmāt ātmanaḥ asambaddha-pralāpi-tva-parihāra-artham kvacid vācyam pramāṇam
_
tat ca-aniścitam vaktum aśakyam tat niścayaḥ ca bhrānta-abhra-anta-viveka-niścaya-abhāvāt na-upapadyate iti atas asti bhrānta-tva-ādi-niścayaḥ
_

##
sa tarhi kutas pramāṇāt iti ced-
_
nanu paryālocanīyam etat āvayoḥ api
_
tatra jñāna-udaya-anantaram eva nirvikalpakam pratyakṣam bhrānta-tva-ādi-viśiṣṭa-jñāna-grāhakam
abhyupagantavyam anyathā hi ayam bhrānta-abhra-anta-vyahāraḥ nirmūlaḥ syāt-
_
na ca viparyastasya-apravṛtti-prasaṅgaḥ
_
tad-bhrānta-tva-grahaṇasya-avyakta-tvāt dhāvataḥ kaṇṭaka-ādi-grahaṇa-vat-
_
vyaktam hi jñānam smaraṇa-ātmakam anubhava-ātmakam vā-aniṣṭa-viṣaya-nivṛtti-nimittam dṛṣṭam
_
avyakta-grahaṇe api nimitta-viśeṣāt vyaktā smṛtiḥ dṛṣṭā
_

##
yathā prathama-gopiṇḍa-grahaṇa-kāle go-tvasya-anugata-vyāvṛtta-svarūpa-avyakta-grahaṇe sati uttara-kālam
sajātīya-vijātīya-piṇḍa-darśanāt spaṣṭā smṛtiḥ bhavati
_
anena sadṛśaḥ mayā dṛṣṭaḥ
_
etat-vilakṣaṇaḥ mayā dṛṣṭa
_
etat sādṛśyam tasya-etat vailakṣaṇyam iti
_
evam bhrānta-tva-abhrānta-tvayoḥ aspaṣṭa-grahaṇa-uttara-kālam bādhaka-saṃvādaka-jñāna-nimittāt spaṣṭā smṛtiḥ bhavati
_
mithyā samyak vā jñānam tat iti
_
atha vā bādhaka-saṃvādaka-jñāna-utpatti-anantaram eva tayoḥ pratyakṣa-tvam
_
tadānīm tayoḥ pratyakṣa-tvam ayuktam iti ced na
_
manasaḥ tri-kāla-viṣaya-tvāt
_

##
anyathā hi yoginām api atīta-anāgata-grahaṇam na syāt-
_
yoginām yoga-ja-dharma-sāmarthyāt atīta-ādi-pratyakṣam yuktam iti ced-
_
nanu asmad-ādeḥ api kvacid viṣaye nimitta-viśeṣa-sahita-pūrva-upārjita-adṛṣṭa-sāmarthyāt kim na yuktam
_
kārya-anumeya-tvāt adṛṣṭa-sāmarthyasya-iti
_
atha bādha-utpāda-ādi-anumānena-eva bhrānta-tva-ādi niścīyate
_
vyāptiḥ tarhi kena gṛhītā
_
nanu anumāna-antareṇa tasya-api anumāna-antareṇa-iti bhūtā tarhi anavasthā
_
tatas kaḥ doṣaḥ
_
saṃsārasya-anādi-tvāt iṣṭā-eva-iti
_
prathamam tāvat āhāra-ādi-smaraṇa-vat vyāpti-smaraṇam api janma-antara-bhāvi-saṃskārāt adṛṣṭa-upetāt bhavati
_

##
dṛṣṭānta-paramparā-sā-dhana-upakṣīṇasya na prakṛta-sādhya-siddhiḥ iti ced-
_
na
_
dṛṣṭāntasya prāk eva siddha-tvāt-
_
na hi kaścid agni-anumāna-kāle mahānasa-ādiṣu agni-dhūmayoḥ sambandham pratyeti
_
atha param pratyanumāna-kāle avaśyam dṛṣṭāntaḥ sādhanīyaḥ iti
_
sa yadi paraḥ sarvatra dṛṣṭāntam prati vipratipadyate
_
tadā-asya tad-mūlaḥ bhrānta-abhra-anta-niścayaḥ na prāpnoti
_
tad-abhāve lokaśāstra-prasiddhaḥ tad-vyavahāraḥ nivarttate
_
dhūma-ādi-vyāpti-grahaṇasya-api apahnavam kurvāṇaḥ hi loka-vyavahāra-virodhena-eva nirākriyate na-anyathā
_

##
sa ca virodhaḥ atra-api samānaḥ
_
tasmāt parasya-api kvacid prasiddhaḥ dṛṣṭāntaḥ tad-balena-anyaḥ api sādhyate iti
_
atha vā ādau tāvat sarva-pratyakṣa-darśinā kenacid asarva-vidaḥ kecid vyāptim grāhitāḥ
_
taiḥ tu āpta-upadeśa-ādi asya jñānasya bādhā bhavati tat-bhrāntam
_
yasya tu saṃvādakam jñāna-antaram bhavati tat-abhra-antam iti evam vyāptim
gṛhītvā-anye grāhitāḥ taiḥ api anye taiḥ api anyaḥ iti evam yāvat asmad-ādayaḥ ghaṭa-ādi-divya-vyāpti-vat iti
_
kārya-kāraṇayoḥ api pratyakṣa-anumānābhyām vyāpti-grahaṇam anayā-eva-upapattyā samarthayitavyam iti
_

##
yat ca-uktam aduṣṭa-sāmagrī-janya-tvam abhrānta-tva-liṅgam na bhavati-iti
_
tat-abhyupagamyate eva
_
bādhā-anutpattiḥ tu gamikā kvacid iṣṭā-eva yatra-avabhāta-artha-aviparīta-tvam
antareṇa-anyathā bādhā-anutpattiḥ na sambhāvyate vicārakaiḥ api yathā deśa-kāla-avasthā-ādi-viśeṣa-upetasya jñātuḥ candra-eka-tva-jñāne
_
yat punar etad-viparyaya-jñānam sva-kāraṇa-sāmarthyāt tathāvidham utpannam
_
yat-bādhaka-jñāna-utpāda-pratibandha-kṛt tat-havaṃsaḥ api tathāvidhaḥ eva
_
pareṣṭa-samyak-jñāna-vat iti
_

##
tat api ayuktam
_
na hi pareṇa samyak-jñānam bādhaka-jñāna-utpāda-pratibandha-kṛt iṣṭam
_
kim tarhi
_
yathā-pratibhāsa-viṣayasya-avaiparītyam bhrānti-anutpādaḥ vā
_
na ca kasyacid utpādasya bhūtasya-abhūtasya vā pratibandhaḥ kartum śakyate
_
kim tarhi
_
kāraṇam pratibadhyate
_
yat kāraṇam sahakāriṣu satsu api kasyacid sadbhāve kāryam na karoti
_
abhāve tu karoti
_
tat tena pratibaddham ucyate
_
yathā guru-tva-ādikam saṃyoga-ādinā
_
bādha-utpattau ca kim kena pratibadhyate iti
_
na ca yat yasya pratibandhakam tat-dhvaṃsaḥ api tasya pratibandhakaḥ bhavati
_
na hi saṃyoga-ādi-dhvaṃsaḥ api guru-tva-ādeḥ pratibandhakaḥ
_
pratyuta sahakāryā-iva-iti
_

##
tasmāt tad-dhvaṃsaḥ api tathāvidhaḥ eva-iti anupapannam
_
śuktikā-iyam rajatam ityādi-jñānasya prāk-utpanna-rajatam idam ityādi-jñāna-bādhaka-tvena loka-śāstreṣu prasiddha-tvāt-
_
tasya kim arthaḥ bādhyate
_
jñānam vā-ityādi-aneka-vikalpaiḥ nirākaraṇam gomaya-pāyasīya-nyāyam ākṣipati
_
tathā ca kā-iyam jñānasya bādhā viṣaya-apahāra-lakṣaṇā phala-apahāra-lakṣaṇā vā-ityādi vicāraḥ api na kvacid upayujyate
_
bhrānti-jñānasya-eva bādhā bhavati na yathārtha-jñānasya-iti evam vyāpti-pratipādanam eva-upayujyate
_
tat evam bādha-anutpādena kvacid abhrānta-tvam gamyate
_
kvacid punar pravṛtti-sāmarthyena yathā-udaka-ādi-artha-prāptyā pūrva-udita-udaka-ādi-jñānasya-iti
_

##
nanu ca pratibhāta-arthasya prāptiḥ na sambhavati-iti uktam
_
na
_
pāṣāṇa-ādi-arthasya kasyacid avabhātasya prāpti-sambhavāt-
_
udaka-ādi-arthasya tu vināśe api tad-saṃtāna-bhāvinaḥ prāptiḥ gamikā
_
kaḥ punar ayam santānaḥ
_
tad-sadṛśa-nirantara-utpādaḥ
_
tat eva-idam iti laukika-avisaṃvādi-pratyaya-hetuḥ
_
na hi bhrāntyā-artham avadhārya pravarttamānaḥ tad-santāna-bhāvi-artham api yathā pratibhānam prāpnoti
_
tasmāt avabhāta-artha-prāptyā tad-santāna-bhāvi-artha-prāptyā ca pūrva-jñānasya-abhrānta-tvam gamyate
_
etena tad-deśa-tvam vyākhyātam
_
tad-aniścaye kutas tad-santāna-bhāvi-tva-niścayaḥ iti ced-
_
na
_
avisaṃvādataḥ tad-niścayāt-
_
na hi avabhāta-arthasya-asambhave anyathātve vā-ayam pratipattā pravṛtti-uttara-kālam
saḥ eva-ayam iti tam artham vyavasāya-anekeṣu jana-samūheṣu vyavaharan api na visaṃvādyate
_
tat evam avisaṃvādāt niścīyate yathā-eva-asau arthaḥ pratibhātaḥ tathā-eva-iti
_

##
yaḥ tu punar apravṛttaḥ api avabhāta-arthasya viśeṣān-upalabhate sa tad-deśa-sthaḥ eva taiḥ viśeṣaiḥ artha-tathā-bhāvam
vyavasāya pūrva-jñānasya-abhra-anta-tvam pratipadyate puruṣa-antara-vacana-saṃvādāt vā-iti
_
evam ca candra-ādi-jñānānām abhrānta-tvam na sidhyet iti uktam
_
na hi pravṛtti-sāmarthyāt eva-iti avadhāryate
_
hutāśana-ādi-vat abhrānta-tvasya-api vikalpena-aneka-liṅga-anumeya-tvāt pramāṇa-antara-gamya-tvāt ca-iti
_

##
anye tu varṇayanti
_
pravṛtti-sāmarthyam iti anena pramāṇa-antara-saṃvādaḥ abhipretaḥ
_
sa ca-avabhāta-arthasya naṣṭasya-anaṣṭasya vā viśeṣa-upalambhāt sambhāvita-aneka-puruṣa-abhidhānāt vā bhavati
_
tena sarvatra-api saṃśaya-viṣaye pravṛtti-sāmarthyāt eva-avyabhicāri-tva-niścayam abhyupagacchatā na doṣaḥ kaścid iti
_
yā punar iyam anavasthā coditā tad-avagateḥ avyabhicāri-tvam katham avagamyate ityādinā
_
sā-api niścaya-pūrvaka-loka-vyavahāra-bādhita-tvāt anupapannā
_
yatas dvitīyena tṛtīyena vā viśeṣa-upalambhena nirākākāṅkṣāḥ vyavahartāraḥ dṛśyante iti
_
prāk eva vā-anavasthā nirākṛtā-iti atas anavasthā-bhayāt svatas-prāmāṇyam api na-abhyupagantavyam
_
