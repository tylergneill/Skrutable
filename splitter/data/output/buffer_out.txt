na svatas na-api paratas na dvābhyām na-api ahetutaḥ
_
utpannāḥ jātu vidyante bhāvāḥ kva cana ke cana
_
catvāraḥ pratyayāḥ hetuḥ ārambaṇam anantaram
_
tathā-eva-ādhipateyam ca pratyayaḥ na-asti pañcamaḥ
_
na hi svabhāvaḥ bhāvānām pratyaya-ādiṣu vidyate
_
avidyamāne svabhāve para-bhāvaḥ na vidyate
_
kriyā na pratyayavatī na-apratyayavatī kriyā
_
pratyayāḥ na-akriyāvantaḥ kriyāvantaḥ ca santi uta

##
utpadyate pratītya-imān iti-ime pratyayāḥ kila
_
yāvat na-utpadyate ime tāvat na-apratyayāḥ katham
_
na-eva-asataḥ na-eva sataḥ pratyayaḥ arthasya yujyate
_
asataḥ pratyayaḥ kasya sataḥ ca pratyayena kim
_
na san na-āsat na sat asan dharmaḥ nirvartate yadā
_
katham nirvartakaḥ hetuḥ evam sati hi yujyate
_
anārambaṇaḥ eva-ayam san dharmaḥ upadiśyate
_
atha-anārambaṇe dharme kutas ārambaṇam punar-
_

##
anutpanneṣu dharmeṣu nirodhaḥ na-upapadyate
_
na-anantaram atas yuktam niruddhe pratyayaḥ ca kaḥ
_
bhāvānām niḥsvabhāvānām na sattā vidyate yatas-
_
sati-idam asmin bhavati-iti etat na-eva-upapadyate
_
na ca vyasta-samasteṣu pratyayeṣu-asti tat phalam
_
pratyayebhyaḥ katham tat ca bhavet na pratyayeṣu yat-
_
atha-asat api tat tebhyaḥ pratyayebhyaḥ pravartate
_
apratyayebhyaḥ api kasmāt na-abhipravartate phalam
_
phalam ca pratyaya-mayam pratyayāḥ-ca-asvayaṃmayāḥ
_
phalam-asvamayebhyaḥ yat-tat-pratyaya-mayam katham
_
tasmāt na pratyaya-mayam na-apratyaya-mayam phalam
_
saṃvidyate phala-abhāvāt pratyaya-apratyayāḥ kutas-

##
gatam na gamyate tāvat agatam na-eva gamyate
_
gatāgata-vinirmuktam gamyamānam na gamyate
_
ceṣṭā yatra gatiḥ tatra gamyamāne ca sā yatas-
_
na gate na-āgate ceṣṭā gamyamāne gatiḥ tatas-
_
gamyamānasya gamanam katham nāma-upapatsyate
_
gamyamānam vigamanam yadā na-eva-upapadyate
_
gamyamānasya gamanam yasya tasya prasajyate
_
ṛte gateḥ gamyamānam gamyamānam hi gamyate

##
gamyamānasya gamane prasaktam gamana-dvayam
_
yena tat gamyamānam ca yat ca-atra gamanam punar-
_
dvau gantārau prasajyete prasakte gamana-dvaye
_
gantāram hi tiraskṛtya gamanam na-upapadyate
_
gantāram ced tiraskṛtya gamanam na-upapadyate
_
gamane asati gantā-atha kutas eva bhaviṣyati
_
gantā na gacchati tāvat agantā na-eva gacchati
_
anyaḥ gantuḥ agantuḥ ca kaḥ tṛtīyaḥ atha gacchati

##
gantā tāvat gacchati-iti katham eva-upapatsyate
_
gamanena vinā gantā yadā na-eva-upapadyate

pakṣaḥ gantā gacchati-iti yasya tasya prasajyate
_
gamanena vinā gantā gantuḥ gamanam icchataḥ
_
gamane dve prasajyete gantā yadi-uta gacchati

_
gate na-ārabhyate gantum gantum na-ārabhyate agate
_
na-ārabhyate gamyamāne gantum ārabhyate kuha

##
na pūrvam gamana-ārambhāt gamyamānam na vā gatam
_
yatra-ārabhyeta gamanam agate gamanam kutas-
_
gatam kim gamyamānam kim agatam kim vikalpyate
_
adṛśyamānaḥ ārambhe gamanasya-eva sarvathā
_
gantā na tiṣṭhati tāvat agantā na-eva tiṣṭhati
_
anyaḥ gantuḥ agantuḥ ca kaḥ tṛtīyaḥ atha tiṣṭhati
_
gantā tāvat tiṣṭhati-iti katham eva-upapatsyate
_
gamanena vinā gantā yadā na-eva-upapadyate

##
na tiṣṭhati gamyamānāt na gatān na-āgatāt api

gamanam saṃpravṛttiḥ ca nivṛttiḥ ca gateḥ samā

yat eva gamanam gantā saḥ eva-iti na yujyate

anyaḥ eva punar gantā gateḥ iti na yujyate

yat eva gamanam gantā saḥ eva hi bhavet yadi

ekībhāvaḥ prasajyeta kartuḥ karmaṇaḥ eva ca
_
anyaḥ eva punar gantā gateḥ yadi vikalpyate
_
gamanam syāt ṛte gantuḥ gantā syāt gamanāt ṛte

##
ekībhāvena vā siddhiḥ nānā-bhāvena vā yayoḥ
_
na vidyate tayoḥ siddhiḥ katham nu khalu vidyate
_
gatyā yayā-ajyate gantā gatim tām sa na gacchati
_
yasmāt na gati-pūrvaḥ asti kaścid kiṃcid dhi gacchati

gatyā yayā-ajyate gantā tatas anyām sa na gacchati

gatī dve na-upapadyete yasmāt eke tu gantari
_
sadbhūtaḥ gamanam gantā tri-prakāram na gacchati

na-asat-bhūtaḥ api gamanam tri-prakāram sa gacchati
_
gamanam sat-asat-bhūtaḥ tri-prakāram na gacchati

tasmāt gatiḥ ca gantā ca gantavyam ca na vidyate

##
darśanam śravaṇam ghrāṇam rasanam sparśanam manaḥ
_
indriyāṇi ṣaṭ eteṣām draṣṭavī-a-ādīni gocaraḥ
_
svam ātmānam darśanam hi tat tam eva na paśyati
_
na paśyati yat ātmānam katham drakṣyati tat parān
_
na paryāptaḥ agni-dṛṣṭāntaḥ darśanasya prasiddhaye
_
sa-darśanaḥ sa pratyuktaḥ gamyamāna-gata-āgataiḥ
_
na-apaśyamānam bhavati yadā kim cana darśanam
_
darśanam paśyati-iti evam katham etat tu yujyate
_

##
paśyati darśanam na-eva na-eva paśyati adarśanam
_
vyākhyātaḥ darśanena-eva draṣṭā ca-api avagamyatām
_
draṣṭā na-asti atiraskṛtya tiraskṛtya ca darśanam
_
draṣṭavyam darśanam ca-eva draṣṭari asati te kutas-
_
pratītya mātā-pitarau yathā-uktaḥ putra-saṃbhavaḥ
_
cakṣū-rūpe pratītyā-evam-uktaḥ vijñāna-saṃbhavaḥ
_
draṣṭavya-darśana-abhāvāt vijñāna-ādi-catuṣṭayam
_
na-asti-iti upādāna-ādīni bhaviṣyanti punar katham
_
vyākhyātam śravaṇam ghrāṇam rasanam sparśanam manaḥ
_
darśanena-eva jānīyāt śrotṛ-śrotavyaka-ādi ca

##
rūpa-kāraṇa-nirmuktam na rūpam upalabhyate
_
rūpeṇa-api na nirmuktam dṛśyate rūpa-kāraṇam
_
rūpa-kāraṇa-nirmukte rūpe rūpam prasajyate
_
āhetukam na ca-asti arthaḥ kaścid āhetukaḥ kva cit-
_
rūpeṇa tu vinirmuktam yadi syāt rūpa-kāraṇam
_
akāryakam kāraṇam syāt na-asti akāryam ca kāraṇam
_
rūpe sati eva rūpasya kāraṇam na-upapadyate
_
rūpe asati eva rūpasya kāraṇam na-upapadyate
_

##
niṣkāraṇam punā rūpam na-eva na-eva-upapadyate
_
tasmāt rūpa-gatān kāṃścid na vikalpān vikalpayet-
_
na kāraṇasya sadṛśam kāryam iti upapadyate
_
na kāraṇasya-asadṛśam kāryam iti upapadyate
_
vedanā-citta-saṃjñānām saṃskārāṇām ca sarvaśas-
_
sarveṣām eva bhāvānām rūpeṇa-eva samaḥ kramaḥ
_
vigrahe yaḥ parīhāram kṛte śūnyatayā vadet-
_
sarvam tasya-aparihṛtam samam sādhyena jāyate
_
vyākhyāne yaḥ upālambham kṛte śūnyatayā vadet-
_
sarvam tasya-anupālabdham samam sādhyena jāyate

##
na-ākāśam vidyate kiṃcid pūrvam ākāśa-lakṣaṇāt-
_
alakṣaṇam prasajyeta syāt pūrvam yadi lakṣaṇāt-
_
alakṣaṇaḥ na kaścid ca bhāvaḥ saṃvidyate kva cit-
_
asati alakṣaṇe bhāve kramatām kuha lakṣaṇam
_
na-alakṣaṇe lakṣaṇasya pravṛttiḥ na sa-lakṣaṇe
_
sa-lakṣaṇa-alakṣaṇābhyām na-api anyatra pravartate
_
lakṣaṇa-asaṃpravṛttau ca na lakṣyam upapadyate
_
lakṣyasya-anupapattau ca lakṣaṇasya-api asaṃbhavaḥ
_

##
tasmāt na vidyate lakṣyam lakṣaṇam na-eva vidyate
_
lakṣya-lakṣaṇa-nirmuktaḥ na-eva bhāvaḥ api vidyate
_
avidyamāne bhāve ca kasya-abhāvaḥ bhaviṣyati
_
bhāva-abhāva-vidharmā ca bhāva-abhāvau avaiti kaḥ
_
tasmāt-na bhāvaḥ na-abhāvaḥ na lakṣyam na-api lakṣaṇam
_
ākāśam ākāśa-samāḥ dhātavaḥ pañca ye apare
_
astitvam ye tu paśyanti nāstitvam ca-alpabuddhayaḥ
_
bhāvānām te na paśyanti draṣṭavya-upaśamam śivam
_

##
rāgāt yadi bhavet pūrvam raktaḥ rāga-tiraskṛtaḥ
_
tam pratītya bhavet rāgaḥ rakte rāgaḥ bhavet sati
_
rakte asati punā rāgaḥ kutas eva bhaviṣyati
_
sati vā-asati vā rāge rakte api eṣa samaḥ kramaḥ
_
saha-eva punar udbhūtiḥ na yuktā rāga-raktayoḥ
_
bhavetām rāga-raktau hi nirapekṣau parasparam
_
na-ekatve sahabhāvaḥ asti na tena-eva hi tat saha
_
pṛthaktve sahabhāvaḥ atha kutas eva bhaviṣyati
_

##
eka-tve sahabhāvaḥ ced syāt sahāyam vinā-api saḥ
_
pṛthaktve sahabhāvaḥ ced syāt sahāyam vinā-api saḥ
_
pṛthaktve sahabhāvaḥ ca yadi kim rāga-raktayoḥ
_
siddhaḥ pṛthak-pṛthagbhāvaḥ sahabhāvaḥ yatas tayoḥ
_
siddhaḥ pṛthak-pṛthagbhāvaḥ yadi vā rāga-raktayoḥ
_
sahabhāvam kimartham tu parikalpayase tayoḥ
_
pṛthak na sidhyati-iti evam sahabhāvam vikāṅkṣasi
_
sahabhāva-prasiddhi-artham pṛthaktvam bhūyas icchasi
_
pṛthak-bhāva-aprasiddheḥ ca sahabhāvaḥ na sidhyati
_
katamasmin pṛthagbhāve sahabhāvam sati-icchasi
_
evam raktena rāgasya siddhiḥ na saha na-asaha
_
rāga-vat sarva-dharmāṇām siddhiḥ na saha na-asaha
_

##
yadi saṃskṛtaḥ utpādaḥ tatra yuktā tri-lakṣaṇī
_
atha-asaṃskṛtaḥ utpādaḥ katham saṃskṛta-lakṣaṇam
_
utpāda-ādyāḥ trayaḥ vyastāḥ na-alam lakṣaṇa-karmaṇi
_
saṃskṛtasya samastāḥ syuḥ ekatra katham ekadā
_
utpāda-sthiti-bhaṅgānām anyat saṃskṛta-lakṣaṇam
_
asti ced anavasthā-evam na-asti ced te na saṃskṛtāḥ
_
utpāda-utpādaḥ utpādaḥ mūla-utpādasya kevalam
_
utpāda-utpādam utpādaḥ maulaḥ janayate punar-

##
utpāda-utpādaḥ utpādaḥ mūla-utpādasya te yadi
_
maulena-ajanitaḥ tam te sa katham janayiṣyati
_
sa te maulena janitaḥ maulam janayate yadi
_
maulaḥ sa tena-ajanitaḥ tam utpādayate katham
_
ayam utpādyamānaḥ te kāmam utpādayet imam
_
yadi-imam utpādayitum ajātaḥ śaknuyāt ayam
_
pradīpaḥ sva-para-ātmānau saṃprakāśayate yathā
_
utpādaḥ sva-para-ātmānau ubhau utpādayet tathā
_

##
pradīpe na-andhakāraḥ asti yatra ca-asau pratiṣṭhitaḥ
_
kim prakāśayate dīpaḥ prakāśaḥ hi tamaḥ-vadhaḥ
_
katham utpadyamānena pradīpena tamaḥ hatam
_
na-utpadyamānaḥ hi tamaḥ pradīpaḥ prāpnute yadā
_
aprāpya-eva pradīpena yadi vā nihatam tamaḥ
_
ihasthaḥ sarva-loka-stham sa tamaḥ nihaniṣyati
_
pradīpaḥ sva-para-ātmānau saṃprakāśayate yadi
_
tamaḥ api sva-para-ātmānau chādayiṣyati asaṃśayam
_

##
anutpannaḥ ayam utpādaḥ sva-ātmānam janayet katham
_
atha-utpannaḥ janayate jāte kim janyate punar-
_
na-utpadyamānam na-utpannam na-anutpannam katham cana
_
utpadyate tat ākhyātam gamyamāna-gata-āgataiḥ
_
utpadyamānam utpattau idam na kramate yadā
_
katham utpadyamānam tu pratītya-utpattim ucyate
_
pratītya yat yat bhavati tat tat śāntam svabhāvataḥ
_
tasmāt utpadyamānam ca śāntam utpattiḥ eva ca

##
yadi kaścid anutpannaḥ bhāvaḥ saṃvidyate kva cit-
_
utpadyeta sa kim tasmin bhāvaḥ utpadyate asati
_
utpadyamānam utpādaḥ yadi ca-utpādayati ayam
_
utpādayet tam utpādam utpādaḥ katamaḥ punar-
_

_
atha-anutpādaḥ utpannaḥ sarvam utpadyatām tathā
_
sataḥ ca tāvat utpattiḥ asataḥ ca na yujyate
_
na sataḥ ca-asataḥ ca-iti pūrvam eva-upapāditam

##
nirudhyamānasya-utpattiḥ na bhāvasya-upapadyate
_
yaḥ ca-anirudhyamānaḥ tu sa bhāvaḥ na-upapadyate
_
na-āsthitaḥ tiṣṭhate bhāvaḥ sthitaḥ bhāvaḥ na tiṣṭhati
_
na tiṣṭhate tiṣṭhamānaḥ kaḥ anutpannaḥ ca tiṣṭhati
_
sthitiḥ nirudhyamānasya na bhāvasya-upapadyate
_
yaḥ ca-anirudhyamānaḥ tu sa bhāvaḥ na-upapadyate
_
jarā-maraṇa-dharmeṣu sarva-bhāveṣu sarvadā
_
tiṣṭhanti katame bhāvāḥ ye jarā-maraṇam vinā
_

##
sthityā-anyayā sthiteḥ sthānam tayā-eva ca na yujyate
_
utpādasya yathā-utpādaḥ na-ātmanā na para-ātmanā
_
nirudhyate na-aniruddham na niruddham nirudhyate
_
tathā nirudhyamānam ca kim ajātam nirudhyate
_
sthitasya tāvat bhāvasya nirodhaḥ na-upapadyate
_
na-asthitasya-api bhāvasya nirodhaḥ upapadyate
_
tayā-eva-avasthayā-avasthā na hi sā-eva nirudhyate
_
anyayā-avasthayā-avasthā na ca-anyā-eva nirudhyate
_

##
yadā-eva sarva-dharmāṇām utpādaḥ na-upapadyate
_
tadā-evam sarva-dharmāṇām nirodhaḥ na-upapadyate
_
sataḥ ca tāvat bhāvasya nirodhaḥ na-upapadyate
_
eka-tve na hi bhāvaḥ ca na-abhāvaḥ ca-upapadyate
_
asataḥ api na bhāvasya nirodhaḥ upapadyate
_
na dvitīyasya śirasaḥ chedanam vidyate yathā
_
na sva-ātmanā nirodhaḥ asti nirodhaḥ na para-ātmanā
_
utpādasya yathā-utpādaḥ na-ātmanā na para-ātmanā
_
utpāda-sthiti-bhaṅgānām asiddheḥ na-asti saṃskṛtam
_
saṃskṛtasya-aprasiddhau ca katham setsyati asaṃskṛtam
_
yathā māyā yathā svapnaḥ gandharvanagaram yathā
_
tathā-utpādaḥ tathā sthānam tathā bhaṅgaḥ udāhṛtam
_

##
sadbhūtaḥ kārakaḥ karma sadbhūtam na karoti-ayam
_
kārakaḥ na-api asat-bhūtaḥ karma-asadbhūtam īhate
_
sadbhūtasya kriyā na-asti karma ca syāt akartṛkam
_
sadbhūtasya kriyā na-asti kartā ca syāt akarmakaḥ
_
karoti yadi asat-bhūtaḥ asat-bhūtam karma kārakaḥ
_
ahetukam bhavet karma kartā ca-ahetukaḥ bhavet-
_
hetau asati kāryam ca kāraṇam ca na vidyate
_
tad-abhāve kriyā kartā kāraṇam ca na vidyate
_

##
dharma-adharmau na vidyete kriyā-ādīnām asaṃbhave
_
dharme ca-asati adharme ca phalam tad-jam na vidyate
_
phale asati na mokṣāya na svargāya-upapadyate
_
mārgaḥ sarva-kriyāṇām ca nairarthakyam prasajyate
_
kārakaḥ sadasat-bhūtaḥ sat-asat kurute na tat-
_
paraspara-viruddham hi sat ca-asat ca-ekatas kutas-
_
satā ca kriyate na-asat na-asatā kriyate ca sat-
_
kartrā sarve prasajyante doṣāḥ tatra te eva hi

##
na-asat-bhūtam na sadbhūtaḥ sadasat-bhūtam eva vā
_
karoti kārakaḥ karma pūrva-uktaiḥ eva hetubhiḥ
_
na-asat-bhūtaḥ api sadbhūtam sadasat-bhūtam eva vā
_
karoti kārakaḥ karma pūrva-uktaiḥ eva hetubhiḥ
_
karoti sat-asat-bhūtaḥ na san na-asat ca kārakaḥ
_
karma tat tu vijānīyāt pūrva-uktaiḥ eva hetubhiḥ
_
pratītya kārakaḥ karma tam pratītya ca kārakam
_
karma pravartate na-anyat paśyāmaḥ siddhi-kāraṇam
_
evam vidyāt upādānam vyutsargāt iti karmaṇaḥ
_
kartuḥ ca karma-kartṛbhyām śeṣān bhāvān vibhāvayet-
_

##
darśana-śravaṇa-ādīni vedanā-ādīni ca-api atha
_
bhavanti yasya prāk ebhyaḥ saḥ asti-iti eke vadanti-uta
_
katham hi avidyamānasya darśana-ādi bhaviṣyati
_
bhāvasya tasmāt prāk ebhyaḥ saḥ asti bhāvaḥ vyavasthitaḥ
_
darśana-śravaṇa-ādibhyaḥ vedanā-ādibhyaḥ eva ca
_
yaḥ prāk vyavasthitaḥ bhāvaḥ kena prajñapyate atha saḥ
_
vinā-api darśana-ādīni yadi ca-asau vyavasthitaḥ
_
amūni api bhaviṣyanti vinā tena na saṃśayaḥ
_

##
ajyate kenacid kaścid kiṃcid kena cit ajyate
_
kutas kiṃcid vinā kaścid kiṃcid kiṃcid vinā kutas-
_
sarvebhyaḥ darśana-ādibhyaḥ kaścid pūrvaḥ na vidyate
_
ajyate darśana-ādinām anyena punar anyadā
_
sarvebhyaḥ darśana-ādibhyaḥ yadi pūrvaḥ na vidyate
_
ekaikasmāt katham pūrvaḥ darśana-ādeḥ sa vidyate
_
draṣṭā saḥ eva sa śrotā saḥ eva yadi vedakaḥ
_
ekaikasmāt bhavet pūrvam evam ca-etat na yujyate

##
draṣṭā-anyaḥ eva śrotā-anyaḥ vedakaḥ anyaḥ punar yadi
_
sati syāt draṣṭari śrotā bahu-tvam ca-ātmanām bhavet-
_
darśana-śravaṇa-ādīni vedanā-ādīni ca-api atha
_
bhavanti yebhyaḥ teṣu eṣa bhūteṣu api na vidyate
_
darśana-śravaṇa-ādīni vedanā-ādīni ca-api atha
_
na vidyate ced yasya sa na vidyante imāni api
_
prāk ca yaḥ darśana-ādibhyaḥ sāṃpratam ca-ūrdhvam eva ca
_
na vidyate asti na-asti-iti nivṛttāḥ tatra kalpanāḥ
_

##
yat indhanam sa ced agniḥ ekatvam kartṛ-karmaṇoḥ
_
anyaḥ ced indhanāt agniḥ indhanāt api ṛte bhavet-
_
nitya-pradīptaḥ eva syāt apradīpana-hetukaḥ
_
punar-ārambha-vaiyarthyam evam ca-akarmakaḥ sati
_
paratra nirapekṣa-tvāt apradīpana-hetukaḥ
_
punar-ārambha-vaiyarthyam nitya-dīptaḥ prasajyate
_
tatra-etat syāt idhyamānam indhanam bhavati-iti ced-
_
kena-idhyatām indhanam tat tāvat-mātram idam yadā
_

##
anyaḥ na prāpsyate apraptaḥ na dhakṣyati adahan punar-
_
na nirvāsyati anirvāṇaḥ sthāsyate vā sva-liṅgavān
_
anyaḥ evandhanāt agniḥ indhanam prāpnuyāt yadi
_
strī saṃprāpnoti puruṣam puruṣaḥ ca striyam yathā
_
anyaḥ eva-indhanāt agniḥ indhanam kāmam āpnuyāt-
_
agni-indhane yadi syātām anyonyena tiraskṛte
_
yadi-indhanam apekṣya-agniḥ apekṣya-agnim yadi-indhanam
_
katarat pūrva-niṣpannam yat apekṣya-agniḥ indhanam
_

##
yadi-indhanam apekṣya-agniḥ agneḥ siddhasya sādhanam
_
evam sati-indhanam ca-api bhaviṣyati niragnikam
_
yaḥ apekṣya sidhyate bhāvaḥ tam eva-apekṣya sidhyati
_
yadi yaḥ apekṣitavyaḥ sa sidhyatām kam apekṣya kaḥ
_
yaḥ apekṣya sidhyate bhāvaḥ saḥ asiddhaḥ apekṣate katham
_
atha-api apekṣate siddhaḥ tu apekṣā-asya na yujyate
_

##
apekṣya-indhanam agniḥ na na-anapekṣya-agniḥ indhanam
_
apekṣya-indhanam agnim na na-anapekṣya-agnim indhanam
_
āgacchati anyatas na-agniḥ indhane agniḥ na vidyate
_
atra-indhane śeṣam uktam gamyamāna-gata-āgataiḥ
_
indhanam punar agniḥ na na-agniḥ anyatra ca-indhanāt-
_
na-agniḥ indhanavān na-agnau indhanāni na teṣu saḥ
_
agni-indhanābhyām vyākhyātaḥ ātma-upādānayoḥ kramaḥ
_
sarvaḥ niravaśeṣeṇa sārdham ghaṭa-paṭa-ādibhiḥ
_
ātmanaḥ ca satattvam ye bhāvānām ca pṛthak pṛthak-
_
nirdiśanti na tān manye śāsanasya-artha-kovidān
_

##
pūrvā prajñāyate koṭiḥ na-iti uvāca mahā-muniḥ
_
saṃsāraḥ anavara-agraḥ hi na-asya-ādiḥ na-api paścimam
_
na-eva-agram na-avaram yasya tasya madhyam kutas bhavet-
_
tasmāt na-atra-upapadyante pūrva-apara-saha-kramāḥ
_
pūrvam jātiḥ yadi bhavet jarā-maraṇam uttaram
_
nirjarā maraṇā jātiḥ bhavet jāyeta ca-amṛtaḥ
_
paścāt jātiḥ yadi bhavet jarā-maraṇam āditas-
_
ahetukam ajātasya syāt jarā-maraṇam katham
_

##
na jarā-maraṇam ca-eva jātiḥ ca saha yujyate
_
mriyeta jāyamānaḥ ca syāt ca-ahetu-katobhayoḥ
_
yatra na prabhavanti ete pūrva-apara-sahakramāḥ
_
prapañcayanti tām jātim tat jarā-maraṇam ca kim
_
kāryam ca kāraṇam ca-eva lakṣyam lakṣaṇam eva ca
_
vedanā vedakaḥ ca-eva santi arthāḥ ye ca ke cana
_
pūrvā na vidyate koṭiḥ saṃsārasya na kevalam
_
sarveṣām api bhāvānām pūrvā koṭī na vidyate
_

##
svayam kṛtam para-kṛtam dvābhyām kṛtam ahetukam
_
duḥkham iti ekaḥ icchanti tat ca kāryam na yujyate
_
svayam kṛtam yadi bhavet pratītya na tatas bhavet-
_
skandhān imān amī skandhāḥ saṃbhavanti pratītya hi
_
yadi amībhyaḥ ime anye syuḥ ebhyaḥ vā-amī pare yadi
_
bhavet para-kṛtam duḥkham paraiḥ ebhiḥ amī kṛtāḥ
_

##
sva-pudgala-kṛtam duḥkham yadi duḥkham punar vinā
_
sva-pudgalaḥ sa katamaḥ yena duḥkham svayam kṛtam
_
para-pudgala-jam duḥkham yadi yasmai pradīyate
_
pareṇa kṛtvā tat duḥkham sa duḥkhena vinā kutas-
_
para-pudgala-jam duḥkham yadi kaḥ para-pudgalaḥ
_
vinā duḥkhena yaḥ kṛtvā parasmai prahiṇoti tat-
_
svayam kṛtasya-aprasiddheḥ duḥkham para-kṛtam kutas-
_
paraḥ hi duḥkham yat kuryāt tat tasya syāt svayam kṛtam
_
na tāvat sva-kṛtam duḥkham na hi tena-eva tat kṛtam
_
paraḥ na-ātma-kṛtaḥ ced syāt duḥkham para-kṛtam katham
_
syāt ubhābhyām kṛtam duḥkham syāt ekaika-kṛtam yadi
_
para-ākāra-asvayaṃkāram duḥkham ahetukam kutas-
_
na kevalam hi duḥkhasya cāturvidhyam na vidyate
_
bāhyānām api bhāvānām cāturvidhyam na vidyate
_

##
tat mṛṣā moṣa-dharma yat bhagavān iti abhāṣata
_
sarve ca moṣa-dharmāṇaḥ saṃskārāḥ tena te mṛṣā
_
tat mṛṣā moṣa-dharma yat yadi kim tatra muṣyate
_
etat tu-uktam bhagavatā śūnyatā-paridīpakam
_
bhāvānām niḥsvabhāva-tvam anyathā-abhāva-darśanāt
_
na-asvabhāvaḥ ca bhāvaḥ asti bhāvānām śūnyatā yatas-
_
kasya syāt anyathā-bhāvaḥ svabhāvaḥ ced na vidyate
_
kasya syāt anyathā-bhāvaḥ svabhāvaḥ yadi vidyate
_

##
tasya-eva na-anyathā-bhāvaḥ na-api anyasya-eva yujyate
_
yuvā na jīryate yasmāt yasmāt jīrṇaḥ na jīryate
_
tasya ced anyathā-bhāvaḥ kṣīram eva bhavet dadhi
_
kṣīrāt anyasya kasyacid dadhi-bhāvaḥ bhaviṣyati
_
yadi aśūnyam bhavet kiṃcid syāt śūnyam iti kim cana
_
na kiṃcid asti aśūnyam ca kutas śūnyam bhaviṣyati
_
śūnyatā sarva-dṛṣṭīnām proktā niḥsaraṇam jinaiḥ
_
yeṣām tu śūnyatā-dṛṣṭiḥ tān asādhyān babhāṣire
_

##
draṣṭavyam darśanam draṣṭā trīṇi etāni dviśas dviśas-
_
sarvaśas ca na saṃsargam anyonyena vrajanti uta
_
evam rāgaḥ ca raktaḥ ca rañjanīyam ca dṛśyatām
_
traidhena śeṣāḥ kleśāḥ ca śeṣāṇi āyatanāni ca
_
anyena-anyasya saṃsargaḥ tat ca-anya-tvam na vidyate
_
draṣṭavya-prabhṛtīnām yat na saṃsargam vrajanti atas-
_

##
na ca kevalam anya-tvam draṣṭavī-a-ādeḥ na vidyate
_
kasyacid kenacid sārdham na-anyatvam upapadyate
_
anyat anyat pratītya-anyat na-anyat anyat ṛte anyatas-
_
yat pratītya ca yat tasmāt tat anyat na-upapadyate
_
yadi anyat anyat anyasmāt anyasmāt api ṛte bhavet-
_
tat anyat anyat anyasmāt ṛte na-asti ca na-asti atas-
_
na-anyasmin vidyate anya-tvam ananyasmin na vidyate
_
avidyamāne ca-anya-tve na-asti anyat vā tat eva vā
_
na tena tasya saṃsargaḥ na-anyena-anyasya yujyate
_
saṃsṛjyamānam saṃsṛṣṭam saṃsraṣṭā ca na vidyate
_

##
na saṃbhavaḥ svabhāvasya yuktaḥ pratyaya-hetubhiḥ
_
hetu-pratyaya-saṃbhūtaḥ svabhāvaḥ kṛtakaḥ bhavet-
_
svabhāvaḥ kṛtakaḥ nāma bhaviṣyati punar katham
_
akṛtrimaḥ svabhāvaḥ hi nirapekṣaḥ paratra ca
_
kutas svabhāvasya-abhāve para-bhāvaḥ bhaviṣyati
_
svabhāvaḥ para-bhāvasya para-bhāvaḥ hi kathyate
_
svabhāva-para-bhāvābhyām ṛte bhāvaḥ kutas punar-
_
svabhāve para-bhāve ca sati bhāvaḥ hi sidhyati
_

##
bhāvasya ced aprasiddhiḥ abhāvaḥ na-eva sidhyati
_
bhāvasya hi anyathābhāvam abhāvam bruvate janāḥ
_
svabhāvam para-bhāvam ca bhāvam ca-abhāvam eva ca
_
ye paśyanti na paśyanti te tattvam buddha-śāsane
_
kātyāyana-avavāde ca-asti-iti na-asti-iti ca-ubhayam
_
pratiṣiddham bhagavatā bhāva-abhāva-vibhāvinā
_
yadi astitvam prakṛtyā syāt na bhavet asya na-astitā
_
prakṛteḥ anyathā-bhāvaḥ na hi jātu-upapadyate
_
prakṛtau kasya vāsatyām anyathātvam bhaviṣyati
_
prakṛtau kasya vā satyām anyathātvam bhaviṣyati
_
asti-iti śāśvata-grāhaḥ na-asti-iti uccheda-darśanam
_
tasmāt astitvanā-astitve na-aśrīyeta vicakṣaṇaḥ
_
asti yat dhi svabhāvena na tat na-asti-iti śāśvatam
_
na-asti-idānīm abhūt pūrvam iti ucchedaḥ prasajyate
_

##
saṃskārāḥ saṃsaranti ced na nityāḥ saṃsaranti te
_
saṃsaranti ca na-anityāḥ sattve api eṣa samaḥ kramaḥ
_
pudgalaḥ saṃsarati ced skandha-āyatana-dhātuṣu
_
pañcadhā mṛgyamāṇaḥ asau na-asti kaḥ saṃsariṣyati
_
upādānāt upādānam saṃsaran vibhavaḥ bhavet-
_
vibhavaḥ ca-anupādānaḥ kaḥ sa kim saṃsariṣyati
_
saṃskārāṇām na nirvāṇam katham cit upapadyate
_
sattvasya-api na nirvāṇam katham cit upapadyate
_

##
na badhyante na mucyante udaya-vyaya-dharmiṇaḥ
_
saṃskārāḥ pūrvavat sattvaḥ badhyate na na mucyate
_
bandhanam ced upādānam sa-upādānaḥ na badhyate
_
badhyate na-anupādānaḥ kimavasthaḥ atha badhyate
_
badhnīyāt bandhanam kāmam bandhyāt pūrvam bhavet yadi
_
na ca-asti tat śeṣam uktam gamyamāna-gata-āgataiḥ
_
baddhaḥ na mucyate tāvat abaddhaḥ na-eva mucyate
_
syātām baddhe mucyamāne yugapad bandha-mokṣaṇe
_
nirvāsyāmi anupādānaḥ nirvāṇam me bhaviṣyati
_
iti yeṣām grahaḥ teṣām upādāna-mahā-grahaḥ
_
na nirvāṇa-samāropaḥ na saṃsāra-apakarṣaṇam
_
yatra kaḥ tatra saṃsāraḥ nirvāṇam kim vikalpyate
_

##
ātma-saṃyamakam cetaḥ para-anugrāhakam ca yat-
_
maitram sa dharamaḥ tat bījam phalasya pretya ca-iha ca
_
cetanā cetayitvā ca karma-uktam parama-ṛṣiṇā
_
tasya-anekavidhaḥ bhedaḥ karmaṇaḥ parikīrtitaḥ
_
tatra yat cetanā-iti uktam karma tat mānasam smṛtam
_
cetayitvā ca yat tu-uktam tat tu kāyika-vācikam
_
vāc-viṣpandaḥ aviratayaḥ yāḥ ca-avijñapti-saṃjñitāḥ
_
avijñaptayaḥ eva-anyāḥ smṛtāḥ viratayaḥ tathā
_

##
paribhoga-anvayam puṇyam apuṇyam ca tathāvidham
_
cetanā ca-iti saptā-ete dharmāḥ karma-añjanāḥ smṛtāḥ
_
tiṣṭhati ā pāka-kālāt ced karma tat nityatām iyāt-
_
niruddham ced niruddham sat kim phalam janayiṣyati
_
yaḥ aṅkura-prabhṛtiḥ bījāt saṃtānaḥ abhipravartate
_
tatas phalam ṛte bījāt sa ca na-abhipravartate
_
bījāt ca yasmāt saṃtānaḥ saṃtānāt ca phala-udbhavaḥ
_
bīja-pūrvam phalam tasmāt na-ucchinnam na-api śāśvatam
_

##
yaḥ tasmāt citta-saṃtānaḥ cetasaḥ abhipravartate
_
tatas phalam ṛte cittāt sa ca na-abhipravartate
_
cittāt ca yasmāt saṃtānaḥ saṃtānāt ca phala-udbhavaḥ
_
karma-pūrvam phalam tasmāt na-ucchinnam na-api śāśvatam
_
dharmasya sādhana-upāyāḥ śuklāḥ karma-pathāḥ daśa
_
phalam kāma-guṇāḥ pañca dharmasya pretya ca-iha ca
_

##
bahavaḥ ca mahāntaḥ ca doṣāḥ syuḥ yadi kalpanā
_
syāt eṣā tena na-eva-eṣā kalpanātra-upapadyate
_
imām punar pravakṣyāmi kalpanām yā-atra yojyate
_
buddhaiḥ pratyekabuddhaiḥ ca śrāvakaiḥ ca-anuvarṇitām
_
pattram yathā avipraṇāśaḥ-tathā-ṛṇam iva karma ca
_
caturvidhaḥ dhātutaḥ sa prakṛtyā-vyākṛtaḥ ca saḥ
_
prahāṇataḥ na praheyaḥ bhāvanā-heyaḥ eva vā
_
tasmāt avipraṇāśena jāyate karmaṇām phalam
_

##
prahāṇataḥ praheyaḥ syāt karmaṇaḥ saṃkrameṇa vā
_
yadi doṣāḥ prasajyeran tatra karma-vadha-ādayaḥ
_
sarveṣām visa-bhāgānām sa-bhāgānām ca karmaṇām
_
pratisaṃdhau sa-dhātūnām ekaḥ utpadyate tu saḥ
_
karmaṇaḥ karmaṇaḥ dṛṣṭe dharmaḥ utpadyate tu saḥ
_
dvi-prakārasya sarvasya vipakve api ca tiṣṭhati
_
phala-vyatikramāt vā sa maraṇāt vā nirudhyate
_
anāsravam sa-āsravam ca vibhāgam tatra lakṣayet-
_

##
śūnyatā ca na ca-ucchedaḥ saṃsāraḥ ca na śāśvatam
_
karmaṇaḥ avipraṇāśaḥ ca dharmaḥ buddhena deśitaḥ
_
karma na-utpadyate kasmāt niḥsvabhāvam yatas tatas-
_
yasmāt ca tat anutpannam na tasmāt vipraṇaśyati
_
karma svabhāvataḥ ced syāt śāśvatam syāt asaṃśayam
_
akṛtam ca bhavet karma kriyate na hi śāśvatam
_

##
akṛta-abhyāgam-abhayam syāt karma-akṛtakam yadi
_
abrahmacarya-vāsaḥ ca doṣaḥ-tatra prasajyate
_
vyavahārāḥ virudhyante sarve eva na saṃśayaḥ
_
puṇya-pāpa-kṛtoḥ na-eva pravibhāgaḥ ca yujyate
_
tat vipakva-vipākam ca punar eva vipakṣyati
_
karma vyavasthitam yasmāt tasmāt svābhāvikam yadi
_
karma kleśa-ātmakam ca-idam te ca kleśāḥ na tattvataḥ
_
na ced te tattvataḥ kleśāḥ karma syāt tattvataḥ katham
_

##
karma kleśāḥ ca dehānām pratyayāḥ samudāhṛtāḥ
_
karma kleśāḥ ca te śūnyā yadi deheṣu kā kathā
_
avidyā-nivṛtaḥ jantuḥ tṛṣṇā-saṃyojanaḥ ca saḥ
_
sa bhoktā sa ca na kartuḥ anyaḥ na ca saḥ eva saḥ
_
na pratyaya-samutpannam na-apratyaya-samutthitam
_
asti yasmāt idam karma tasmāt kartā-api na-asti atas-
_
karma ced na-asti kartā ca kutas syāt karma-jam phalam
_
asatī atha phale bhoktā kutas eva bhaviṣyati
_
yathā nirmitakam śāstā nirmimīta rddhi-saṃpadā
_
nirmitaḥ nirmimīta-anyam sa ca nirmitakaḥ punar-
_
tathā nirmitaka-ākāraḥ kartā yat karma tat kṛtam
_
tat-yathā nirmitena-anyaḥ nirmitaḥ nirmitaḥ tathā
_
kleśāḥ karmāṇi dehāḥ ca kartāraḥ ca phalāni ca
_
gandharvanagara-ākārāḥ marīci-svapna-saṃnibhāḥ
_

##
ātmā skandhā yadi bhavet udayavyaya-bhāj bhavet-
_
skandhebhyaḥ anyaḥ yadi bhavet bhavet askandha-lakṣaṇaḥ
_
ātmani asati ca-ātmīyam kutas eva bhaviṣyati
_
nirmamaḥ nirahaṃkāraḥ śamāt ātma-ātmanīnayoḥ
_
nirmamaḥ nirahaṃkāraḥ yaḥ ca saḥ api na vidyate
_
nirmamam nirahaṃkāram yaḥ paśyati na paśyati
_
mama-iti aham iti kṣīṇe bahirdhā-adhyātmam eva ca
_
nirudhyate upādānam tad-kṣayāt janmanaḥ kṣayaḥ
_

##
karma-kleśa-kṣayāt mokṣaḥ karma-kleśāḥ vikalpataḥ
_
te prapañcāt prapañcaḥ tu śūnyatāyām nirudhyate
_
ātmā-iti api prajñapitam anātmā-iti api deśitam
_
buddhaiḥ na-ātmā na ca-anātmā kaścid iti api deśitam
_
nivṛttam abhidhātavyam nivṛttaḥ citta-gocaraḥ
_
anutpanna-aniruddhā hi nirvāṇam iva dharma-tā
_
sarvam tathyam na vā tathyam tathyam ca-atathyam eva ca
_
na-eva-atathyam na-eva tathyam etat buddha-anuśāsanam
_

##
apara-pratyayam śāntam prapañcaiḥ aprapañcitam
_
nirvikalpam anāna-artham etat tattvasya lakṣaṇam
_
pratītya yat yat bhavati na hi tāvat tat eva tat-
_
na ca-anyat-api tat-tasmāt-na-ucchinnam na-api śāśvatam
_
aneka-artham anāna-artham anucchedam aśāśvatam
_
etat tat lokanāthānām buddhānām śāsana-amṛtam
_
saṃbuddhānām anutpāde śrāvakāṇām punar kṣaye
_
jñānam pratyekabuddhānām asaṃsargāt pravartate
_

##
pratyutpannaḥ anāgataḥ ca yadi atītam apekṣya hi
_
pratyutpannaḥ anāgataḥ ca kāle atīte bhaviṣyataḥ
_
pratyutpannaḥ anāgataḥ ca na staḥ tatra punar yadi
_
pratyutpannaḥ anāgataḥ ca syātām katham apekṣya tam
_
anapekṣya punar siddhiḥ na-atītam vidyate tayoḥ
_
pratyutpannaḥ anāgataḥ ca tasmāt kālaḥ na vidyate
_
etena-eva-avaśiṣṭau dvau krameṇa parivartakau
_
uttama-adhama-madhya-ādīn eka-tva-ādīn ca lakṣayet-
_
na-āsthitaḥ gṛhyate kālaḥ sthitaḥ kālaḥ na vidyate
_
yaḥ gṛhyeta-agṛhītaḥ ca kālaḥ prajñapyate katham
_
bhāvam pratītya kālaḥ ced kālaḥ bhāvāt ṛte kutas-
_
na ca kaḥ cana bhāvaḥ asti kutas kālaḥ bhaviṣyati
_

##
hetoḥ ca pratyayānām ca sāmagryā jāyate yadi
_

nirvāṇam yatra bhāvaḥ na na-abhāvaḥ tatra vidyate
_
yadi abhāvaḥ ca nirvāṇam anupādāya tat katham
_
nirvāṇam na hi abhāvaḥ asti yaḥ anupādāya vidyate
_

##
yaḥ ājavaṃjavībhāvaḥ upādāya pratītya vā
_
saḥ apratītya-anupādāya nirvāṇam upadiśyate
_
prahāṇam ca-abravīt śāstā bhavasya vibhavasya ca
_
tasmāt na bhāvaḥ na-abhāvaḥ nirvāṇam iti yujyate
_
bhavet abhāvaḥ bhāvaḥ ca nirvāṇam ubhayam yadi
_
bhavet abhāvaḥ bhāvaḥ ca mokṣaḥ tat ca na yujyate
_
bhavet abhāvaḥ bhāvaḥ ca nirvāṇam ubhayam yadi
_
na-anupādāya nirvāṇam upādāya-ubhayam hi tat-
_

##
bhavet abhāvaḥ bhāvaḥ ca nirvāṇam ubhayam katham
_
asaṃskṛtam hi nirvāṇam bhāva-abhāvau ca saṃskṛtau
_
bhavet abhāvaḥ bhāvaḥ ca nirvāṇaḥ ubhayam katham
_
tayoḥ abhāvaḥ hi ekatra prakāśa-tamasoḥ iva
_
na-eva-abhāvaḥ na-eva bhāvaḥ nirvāṇam iti yā añjanā
_
abhāve ca-eva bhāve ca sā siddhe sati sidhyati
_
na-eva-abhāvaḥ na-eva bhāvaḥ nirvāṇam yadi vidyate
_
na-eva-abhāvaḥ na-eva bhāvaḥ iti kena tat ajyate
_

##
param nirodhāt bhagavān bhavati-iti eva na-ajyate
_
na bhavati ubhayam ca-iti na-ubhayam ca-iti na-ajyate
_
tiṣṭhamānaḥ api bhagavān bhavati-iti eva na-ajyate
_
na bhavati ubhayam ca-iti na-ubhayam ca-iti na-ajyate
_
na saṃsārasya nirvāṇāt kiṃcid asti viśeṣaṇam
_
na nirvāṇasya saṃsārāt kiṃcid asti viśeṣaṇam
_
nirvāṇasya ca yā koṭiḥ koṭiḥ saṃsaraṇasya ca
_
na tayoḥ antaram kiṃcid su-sūkṣmam api vidyate
_

##
param nirodhāt anta-ādyāḥ śāśvata-ādyāḥ ca dṛṣṭayaḥ
_
nirvāṇam apara-antam ca pūrva-antam ca samāśritāḥ
_
śūnyeṣu sarva-dharmeṣu kim anantam kim antavat-
_
kim anantam antavat ca na-anantam na-anta-vat ca kim
_
kim tat eva kim anyat kim śāśvatam kim aśāśvatam
_
aśāśvatam śāśvatam ca kim vā na-ubhayam api atha
_
sarva-upalambha-upaśamaḥ prapañca-upaśamaḥ śivaḥ
_
na kva cit kasyacid kaścid dharmaḥ buddhena deśitaḥ
_

##
punarbhavāya saṃskārān avidyā-nivṛtaḥ tridhā
_
abhisaṃskurute yān taiḥ gatim gacchati karmabhiḥ
_
vijñānam saṃniviśate saṃskāra-pratyayam gatau
_
saṃniviṣṭe atha vijñāne nāma-rūpam niṣicyate
_
niṣikte nāma-rūpe tu ṣaṣ-āyatana-saṃbhavaḥ
_
ṣaṣ-āyatanam āgamya saṃsparśaḥ saṃpravartate
_
cakṣuḥ pratītya rūpam ca samanvāhāram eva ca
_
nāma-rūpam pratītya-evam vijñānam saṃpravartate
_

##
saṃnipātaḥ trayāṇām yaḥ rūpa-vijñāna-cakṣuṣām
_
sparśaḥ sa tasmāt sparśāt ca vedanā saṃpravartate
_
vedanā-pratyayā tṛṣṇā vedanā-artham hi tṛṣyate
_
tṛṣyamāṇaḥ upādānam upādatte caturvidham
_
upādāne sati bhavaḥ upādātuḥ pravartate
_
syāt dhi yadi anupādānaḥ mucyeta na bhavet bhavaḥ
_
pañca skandhāḥ sa ca bhavaḥ bhavāt jātiḥ pravartate
_
jarā-maraṇa-duḥkha-ādi śokāḥ sa-paridevanāḥ
_

##
daurmanasyam upāyāsāḥ jāteḥ etat pravartate
_
kevalasya-evam etasya duḥkha-skandhasya saṃbhavaḥ
_
saṃsāra-mūlam saṃskārān avidvān saṃskaroti atas-
_
avidvān kārakaḥ tasmāt na vidvān tattva-darśanāt
_
avidyāyām niruddhāyām saṃskārāṇām asaṃbhavaḥ
_
avidyāyāḥ nirodhaḥ tu jñānasya-asya-eva bhāvanāt-
_
tasya tasya nirodhena tat tat na-abhipravartate
_
duḥkha-skandhaḥ kevalaḥ ayam evam samyak nirudhyate
_

##
abhūm atītam adhvānam na-abhūvam iti dṛṣṭayaḥ
_
yāḥ tāḥ śāśvata-loka-ādyāḥ pūrva-antam samupāśritāḥ
_
dṛṣṭayaḥ na bhaviṣyāmi kim anyaḥ anāgate adhvani
_
bhaviṣyāmi-iti ca-antādyāḥ apara-antam samāśritāḥ
_
abhūm atītam adhvānam iti etat na-upapadyate
_
yaḥ hi janmasu pūrveṣu saḥ eva na bhavati ayam
_
saḥ eva-ātmā-iti tu bhavet upādānam viśiṣyate
_
upādāna-vinirmuktaḥ ātmā te katamaḥ punar-
_

##
upādāna-vinirmuktaḥ na-asti ātmā-iti kṛte sati
_
syāt upādānam eva-ātmā na-asti ca-ātmā-iti vaḥ punar-
_
na ca-upādānam eva-ātmā vyeti tat samudeti ca
_
katham hi nāma-upādānam upādātā bhaviṣyati
_
anyaḥ punar upādānāt ātmā na-eva-upapadyate
_
gṛhyeta hi anupādānaḥ yadi anyaḥ na ca gṛhyate
_
evam na-anyaḥ upādānāt na ca-upādānam eva saḥ
_
ātmā na-asti anupādānaḥ na-api na-asti eṣa niścayaḥ
_

##
nābhūm atītam adhvānam iti etat na-upapadyate
_
yaḥ hi janmasu pūrveṣu tatas anyaḥ na bhavati ayam
_
yadi hi ayam bhavet anyaḥ pratyākhyāya-api tam bhavet-
_
tathā-eva ca sa saṃtiṣṭhet tatra jāyeta ca-amṛtaḥ
_
ucchedaḥ karmaṇām nāśaḥ kṛtam anyena karma ca
_
pratisaṃvedayet anyaḥ evam ādi prasajyate
_
na-api abhūtvā samudbhūtaḥ doṣaḥ hi atra prasajyate
_
kṛtakaḥ vā bhavet ātmā saṃbhūtaḥ vā-api ahetukaḥ
_

##
evam dṛṣtiḥ atīte yā nābhūm aham abhūm aham
_
ubhayam na-ubhayam ca-iti na-eṣā samupapadyate
_
adhvani anāgate kim nu bhaviṣyāmi-iti darśanam
_
na bhaviṣyāmi ca-iti etat atītena-adhvanā samam
_
sa devaḥ sa manuṣyaḥ ced evam bhavati śāśvatam
_
anutpannaḥ ca devaḥ syāt jāyate na hi śāśvatam
_
devāt anyaḥ manuṣyaḥ ced aśāśvatam atas bhavet-
_
devāt anyaḥ manuṣyaḥ ced saṃtatiḥ na-upapadyate
_

##
divyaḥ yadi eka-deśaḥ syāt eka-deśaḥ ca mānuṣaḥ
_
aśāśvatam śāśvatam ca bhavet tat ca na yujyate
_
aśāśvatam śāśvatam ca prasiddham ubhayam yadi
_
siddhe na śāśvatam kāmam na-eva-aśāśvatam iti api
_
kutaścid āgataḥ kaścid kiṃcid gacchet punar kva cit-
_
yadi tasmāt anādiḥ tu saṃsāraḥ syāt na ca-asti saḥ
_
na-asti ced śāśvataḥ kaścid kaḥ bhaviṣyati aśāśvataḥ
_
śāśvataḥ aśāśvataḥ ca-api dvābhyām ābhyām tiraskṛtaḥ
_

##
antavān yadi lokaḥ syāt para-lokaḥ katham bhavet-
_
atha-api anantavān lokaḥ para-lokaḥ katham bhavet-
_
skandhānām eṣa saṃtānaḥ yasmāt dīpa-arciṣām iva
_
tasmāt na-anantavat-tvam ca na-antavat-tvam ca yujyate
_
pūrve yadi ca bhajyeran utpadyeran na ca-api amī
_
skandhāḥ skandhān pratītya-imān atha lokaḥ antavān bhavet-
_

##
pūrve yadi na bhajyeran utpadyeran na ca-api-amī
_
skandhāḥ skandhān pratītya-imāt lokaḥ anantaḥ bhavet atha
_
antavān eka-deśaḥ ced eka-deśaḥ tu anantavān
_
syāt antavān anantaḥ ca lokaḥ tat ca na yujyate
_
katham tāvat upādātuḥ eka-deśaḥ vinaṅkṣyate
_
na naṅkṣyate ca-eka-deśaḥ evam ca-etat na yujyate
_
upādāna-eka-deśaḥ ca katham nāma vinaṅkṣyate
_
na naṅkṣyate ca-eka-deśaḥ na-etat api upapadyate
_
antavat ca-api anantam ca prasiddham ubhayam yadi
_
siddhe na-eva-anta-vat kāmam na-eva-ananta-vat iti api
_
atha vā sarva-bhāvānām śūnya-tvāt śāśvata-ādayaḥ
_
kva kasya katamāḥ kasmāt saṃbhaviṣyanti dṛṣṭayaḥ
_
sarva-dṛṣṭi-prahāṇāya yaḥ saddharmam adeśayat-
_
anukampām upādāya tam namasyāmi gautamam
_
