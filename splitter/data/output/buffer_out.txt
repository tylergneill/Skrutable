##
tad-liṅgaka-anumānam ca-ayam mantraḥ varhi=-chedane viniyuktaḥ asati bādhake tad-prakāśaka-tvāt-

kravyādaṃśa-prakṣepa-prakāśaka-tad-viniyukta kravyādam-agnim-ityādi-mantra-vat-ityādi

na tu laiṅgikaḥ

śrutiḥ-ca vibhakti-anta-pada-śravaṇam

tad-anantaram-eva ca tādṛśa-jñānāt-indra-upasthāpana-karaṇa tu-asiddhiḥ-bhaviṣyati

tat-pūrvam-eva mantra-adhīna-indra-bodha-nirapekṣayā śrutyā gārhapatya-upasthāpana-karaṇa-tvam bodhyate
_
evañca viniyoga-ākāṅkṣā-virahāt-na laiṅgikaḥ viniyogaḥ-ca sidhyati
_
tat-uktam pārtha-sārathi-miśraiḥ śruti-liṅga-adhikaraṇe yāvat-eva hi mantra-arthaḥ mantreṇa pratipādyate
_
tāvat-eva śrutiḥ-mantram gārhapatya-artha-tām nayet iti
_
etat-ca-aindrī-padam ṛc-viśeṣe rūḍham-iti-āśayena tasya yogena-indra-prakāśaka-tvā-arthakattve aindrī-pada-ādeḥ-asāmarthya-rūpa-liṅga-jñānāt
tatra mantra-janya-śābda-bodha-apekṣā-viraheṇa-asaṅgatya-āpatteḥ

sūtra-uktam śruti-apekṣayā liṅgasya vilambena-artha-bodhaka-tayā daurbalyam-api nirākāṅkṣa-tayā viniyoga-sādhaka-tvam-eva

##
anyonya-viṣaya-niyamaḥ-tu-eka-vākyataḥ iti kārikayā ukta-pūrva-pakṣam-āśaṃkya satyam vākya-virodhaḥ asti śrutitaḥ api virudhyate

sthānam tat-sānnidhyam

samākhyāḥ avayava-śaktyā upayoga-artha-samarthanam

balavat-pratyakṣa-siddha-śruti-ādi-virodha-sthale na tad-viruddha-arthaka-śruti-kalpana-sambhavaḥ

##
tad-virodhena tad-viruddha-arthe liṅga-ādeḥ-apravṛtteḥ
_
pratyakṣa-śruti-viruddhe yathāśruta-arthe smṛteḥ-iva-iti dhyeyam
_
eteṣām-udāharaṇa-ādīni-anyatra-anusandheyāni
_
viniyoga-vidhiḥ-api apūrva-vidhi-niyama-vidhi-parisaṃkhyā-vidhi-bhedāt tridhā

yam vidhim vinā katham-api tad-artha-gocara-pravṛttiḥ-na-utpadyate asau=-apūrva-vidhiḥ
_
sva-viṣayāt-anyatra pravṛtti-virodhī vidhiḥ parisaṃkhyā-vidhiḥ
_
tat-uktam vidhiḥ-atyantam-aprāptau parisaṃkhyā-iti gīyate
_
atyantam-aprāptau=-iti satyām-iti śeṣaḥ
_
satī-iti-agrimam-eva vā vipariṇamayya-atrā-api yojyam
_
atyanta-padañca na-aprāpti-viśeṣaṇa-param bhinna-vibhaktika-tvāt kin tu satyām-iti-atra yaḥ asadhātuḥ-tad-artha-bhavana-rūpa-kriyā-viśeṣaṇa-param
_
atas dvitīya-anutā
_
aprāpti-bhavana-viśeṣaṇam phalataḥ aprāpti-viśeṣaṇam-eva

sa tu pravartakaḥ apūrva-vidhiḥ-iti-arthaḥ
_
evambhūteḥ-ca vidhi-vrīhīn prokṣati-ityādiḥ
_
vrīhi-sādhya-puroḍāśa-dravya-ka-yāga-janyā-pūrva-ādi-arthinām

_

_
pravṛtteḥ-aniyame sati-iti yāvat-
_

_
etasmāt-ca vidhitaḥ prathamam-aśva-rasanā-grahaṇe etad-mantra-viniyoga-lābhe nirākāṅkṣa-tayā gardabha-rasanā-grahaṇe
tad-viniyoga-alābhaḥ iti vaiyarthyena na tat-ācaraṇam-iti etat-avidheḥ-vidhasya-iyatara-uddeśye sambandha-prasañjaka-pravṛtti-virodhitā
_
dvitīyaḥ-ca gṛhamedhīye āghāra-ājya-bhāgau juhoti-iti atra-atideśena prakṛtībhūta-darśa-aṅgasya
prayājāt-eḥ-āghārājya-bhāga-homasya gṛhamedhīya-agnau=-uddeśye sambandha-lābhāt-
_
īdṛśa-vidhi-asattve prayāja-ādi-homasya-api tatra-anuṣṭhānam syāt-
_
vidhi-sattve ca-iti-kartavya-tā-antargatasya-ājya-bhāga-homasya vidhānena-itikartavyatā-antarbhūta homa-samudaya-ākāṅkṣā-anutpattyā

##

parisaṃkhyātaḥ viśeṣaḥ imām-agṛbhṇan rasanām-ityādi-parisaṃkhyāyāḥ aśva-rasana-ādāne
mantra-pāṭha-nivṛtti-niyamasya mantra-liṅgena mantrasya-asādha-araṇyena-upakāraka-tā-bodhanāt-eva nirvāhāt-
tasyāḥ pravṛtti-niyama-anupapādaka-tvāt-iti vibhāvyate
_
tadā-astu parisaṃkhyāyām niyama-anantarbhāvaḥ
_

_
śeṣa-sattvena-asya-aṅgatā-iti āvaśya-ka-upa-vāsa-ādi-dine śeṣa-bhojanam-antareṇa-api pradhāna-nirvāhaḥ-tatra
śrāddhe niḥśeṣa-anna-māṃsa-ādi-dānena śrāddha-śeṣbhaḥ-jana-rūpa-aṅgasya-anapekṣā-nirvāhāt
_
evam-eva śrāddha-viveka-kṛtaḥ
_
na ca śrāddha-janya-paramā-pūrve śeṣa-bhojanasya kāraṇa-tve katham tat-aghaṭita-sāmagrītaḥ paramāpūrva-utpattiḥ
_
kāraṇa-tva-abhāve śeṣa-sattve api tad-apekṣā na syāt katham vā tad-aṅgam bhavet-
_
sa-avaśeṣa-śrāddha-janya-paramā-pūrve tasya kāraṇa-tva-upagame api
niravaśeṣa-śrāddha-janya-parama-apūrva-sāmagryā śeṣa-sattve api tad-bhojanam-antareṇa-api akṣata-tvāt-
pradhāna-anirvāhaḥ durghaṭaḥ itivācyam
_
śeṣa-bhojana-vat śeṣa-abhāvasya-api vaikalpikā-aṅga-tvena śrāddha-janya-parama-apūrva-sāmānya-utpattau
śeṣa-abhāva śeṣa-bhojana-rūpa-aṅga-dvaya-anyatara-sāmagrī-apekṣayā sāmañjasyāt-
_
na ca śeṣa-abhāvasya-aṅga-tā-bodhaka-vidhi-abhāvāt katham tasya-aṅga-tā-iti vācyam
_
śeṣa-sattve kim kurvīta-iti-ākāṅkṣyā pravṛtkasya śeṣa-annam
bhuñjīta-itividheḥ śeṣa-sattve bhojana-apekṣā-bodhaka-tayā śeṣa-asattve tad-anapekṣā-bodhaka-tayā śeṣa-asattve śeṣa-bhojanam
vinā-eva pradhāna-siddhiḥ-iti-etat-lābhāt-
_
tatra śeṣa-abhāvasya-anapekṣāyām
śeṣa-sattve api śeṣa-bhojanam-antareṇa-apūrvasya durvāra-tayā śeṣa-bhojana-vidheḥ-tatra niyamena pravartaka-tva-ayogāt-
vidhi-vaiyarthyena śeṣa-abhāvasya-aṅga-tāyāḥ-tat-bodhaka
vidheḥ-ca-ākṣepāt-ubhaya-sthala-sādhāraṇaḥ śeṣa-bhojana-abhāva-viśiṣṭasya śeṣasya-abhāvaḥ eva-aṅgam
_
bhuñjīta pitṛ-sevitam-iti-anena viśeṣaṇa-abhāva-rūpasya śeṣa-bhojanasya vastu-gatyā viśiṣṭa-abhāva-tva-avacchinnam
yat-apūrva-sādhana-tvam tat-eva bodhyate
_
tad-rūpasya janakatā-avaccheda-kañca vyabhicārāt-
_
śeṣa-bhojana-tvasya tathā-tva-bādhāt tad-uttara-kāla-kalpyam
_
tāvatā viśeṣya-abhāvasya-api sādhāraṇa-rūpeṇa-aṅgatā-siddhiḥ-iti-api-āhuḥ
_

##
īdṛśa-rī-ityeva lohitam
nirasyati śakṛt-nirasyati-ityādi-vidhi-bodhita-lohita śakṛt-nirāsa-ātmaka-pratipatti-rūpa-aṅga-bādhe
api lohita śakṛt-ādya-sattva-sthale pradhāna-siddhi-rūpa-pādanīyā
_
na ca-aṅga-bādha-bhayena lohita-ādi-a-sattva-sthale lohita-ādi-anantaram sampādya-etat-nirasanam yuktam-iti vācyam
_
lohita-ādi-sattve kim kurvīta-iti-ākāṅkṣayā tādṛśa-vidhīnām
pravṛttyā lohita-ādi-asattva-sthale teṣām vidhīnām-apravṛtteḥ-tatra tad-aṅga-tā-bodhaka-abhāvāt
_
etat-nyāya-mūlakaḥ eva pratipatteḥ pratipādya-aprayojaka-tvam-iti pravādaḥ
_

##
atha laukika-śeṣa-bhojanasya-alaukika-aṅga-tve pūrva-divasīya-nirāmiṣa-sakṛt-bhojanasya-api śrāddha-aṅga-tā syāt-iti nirāmiṣam
sakṛt bhuktvā ityādau nirāmiṣa-sakṛt-bhojana-atirikta-bhojana-abhāva-paratvam bhajyeta-iti ced-na
_
pūrva-divase āvaśya-ka-upa-vāsa-sthale āvaśyaka-śrāddhasya-anirvāha-prasaṅgena tatra vaikalpikā-aṅga-antara-kalpane ca
gauraveṇa tad-vacanasya nirāmiṣa-sakṛt-bhojana-ātirikta bhojana-abhāva-rūpa-aṅga-vidhāyaka-tayā eva-upagamāt-iti śivam
_
