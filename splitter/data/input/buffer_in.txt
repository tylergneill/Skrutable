##

##
bhāvasya ced aprasiddhir abhāvo naiva sidhyati

 svabhāvaṃ parabhāvaṃ ca bhāvaṃ cābhāvam eva ca

 na tayor antaraṃ kiṃcit susūkṣmam api vidyate 
_

##
paraṃ nirodhād antādyāḥ śāśvatādyāś ca dṛṣṭayaḥ 
_
 nirvāṇam aparāntaṃ ca pūrvāntaṃ ca samāśritāḥ 
_
 śūnyeṣu sarvadharmeṣu kim anantaṃ kim antavat 
_
 kim anantam antavac ca nānantaṃ nāntavac ca kim 
_
 kiṃ tad eva kim anyat kiṃ śāśvataṃ kim aśāśvatam 
_
 aśāśvataṃ śāśvataṃ ca kiṃ vā nobhayam apy atha 
_
 sarvopalambhopaśamaḥ prapañcopaśamaḥ śivaḥ 
_
 na kva cit kasyacit kaścid dharmo buddhena deśitaḥ 
_

##
punarbhavāya saṃskārān avidyānivṛtas tridhā 
_
 abhisaṃskurute yāṃs tair gatiṃ gacchati karmabhiḥ 
_
 vijñānaṃ saṃniviśate saṃskārapratyayaṃ gatau 
_
 saṃniviṣṭe 'tha vijñāne nāmarūpaṃ niṣicyate 
_
 niṣikte nāmarūpe tu ṣaḍāyatanasaṃbhavaḥ 
_
 ṣaḍāyatanam āgamya saṃsparśaḥ saṃpravartate 
_
 cakṣuḥ pratītya rūpaṃ ca samanvāhāram eva ca 
_
 nāmarūpaṃ pratītyaivaṃ vijñānaṃ saṃpravartate 
_

##
saṃnipātas trayāṇāṃ yo rūpavijñānacakṣuṣām 
_
 sparśaḥ sa tasmāt sparśāc ca vedanā saṃpravartate 
_
 vedanāpratyayā tṛṣṇā vedanārthaṃ hi tṛṣyate 
_
 tṛṣyamāṇa upādānam upādatte caturvidham 
_
 upādāne sati bhava upādātuḥ pravartate 
_
 syād dhi yady anupādāno mucyeta na bhaved bhavaḥ 
_
 pañca skandhāḥ sa ca bhavo bhavāj jātiḥ pravartate 
_
 jarāmaraṇaduḥkhādi śokāḥ saparidevanāḥ 
_

##
daurmanasyam upāyāsā jāter etat pravartate 
_
 kevalasyaivam etasya duḥkhaskandhasya saṃbhavaḥ 
_
 saṃsāramūlaṃ saṃskārān avidvān saṃskaroty ataḥ 
_
 avidvān kārakas tasmān na vidvāṃs tattvadarśanāt 
_
 avidyāyāṃ niruddhāyāṃ saṃskārāṇām asaṃbhavaḥ 
_
 avidyāyā nirodhas tu jñānasyāsyaiva bhāvanāt 
_
 tasya tasya nirodhena tat tan nābhipravartate 
_
 duḥkhaskandhaḥ kevalo 'yam evaṃ samyag nirudhyate 
_

##
abhūm atītam adhvānaṃ nābhūvam iti dṛṣṭayaḥ 
_
 yās tāḥ śāśvatalokādyāḥ pūrvāntaṃ samupāśritāḥ 
_
 dṛṣṭayo na bhaviṣyāmi kim anyo 'nāgate 'dhvani 
_
 bhaviṣyāmīti cāntādyā aparāntaṃ samāśritāḥ 
_
 abhūm atītam adhvānam ity etan nopapadyate 
_
 yo hi janmasu pūrveṣu sa eva na bhavaty ayam 
_
 sa evātmeti tu bhaved upādānaṃ viśiṣyate 
_
 upādānavinirmukta ātmā te katamaḥ punaḥ 
_

##
upādānavinirmukto nāsty ātmeti kṛte sati 
_
 syād upādānam evātmā nāsti cātmeti vaḥ punaḥ 
_
 na copādānam evātmā vyeti tat samudeti ca 
_
 kathaṃ hi nāmopādānam upādātā bhaviṣyati 
_
 anyaḥ punar upādānād ātmā naivopapadyate 
_
 gṛhyeta hy anupādāno yady anyo na ca gṛhyate 
_
 evaṃ nānya upādānān na copādānam eva saḥ 
_
 ātmā nāsty anupādāno nāpi nāsty eṣa niścayaḥ 
_

##
nābhūm atītam adhvānam ity etan nopapadyate 
_
 yo hi janmasu pūrveṣu tato 'nyo na bhavaty ayam 
_
 yadi hy ayaṃ bhaved anyaḥ pratyākhyāyāpi taṃ bhavet 
_
 tathaiva ca sa saṃtiṣṭhet tatra jāyeta cāmṛtaḥ 
_
 ucchedaḥ karmaṇāṃ nāśaḥ kṛtam anyena karma ca 
_
 pratisaṃvedayed anya evam ādi prasajyate 
_
 nāpy abhūtvā samudbhūto doṣo hy atra prasajyate 
_
 kṛtako vā bhaved ātmā saṃbhūto vāpy ahetukaḥ 
_

##
evaṃ dṛṣtir atīte yā nābhūm aham abhūm aham 
_
 ubhayaṃ nobhayaṃ ceti naiṣā samupapadyate 
_
 adhvany anāgate kiṃ nu bhaviṣyāmīti darśanam 
_
 na bhaviṣyāmi cety etad atītenādhvanā samam 
_
 sa devaḥ sa manuṣyaś ced evaṃ bhavati śāśvatam 
_
 anutpannaś ca devaḥ syāj jāyate na hi śāśvatam 
_
 devād anyo manuṣyaś ced aśāśvatam ato bhavet 
_
 devād anyo manuṣyaś cet saṃtatir nopapadyate 
_

##
divyo yady ekadeśaḥ syād ekadeśaś ca mānuṣaḥ 
_
 aśāśvataṃ śāśvataṃ ca bhavet tac ca na yujyate 
_
 aśāśvataṃ śāśvataṃ ca prasiddham ubhayaṃ yadi 
_
 siddhe na śāśvataṃ kāmaṃ naivāśāśvatam ity api 
_
 kutaścid āgataḥ kaścit kiṃcid gacchet punaḥ kva cit 
_
 yadi tasmād anādis tu saṃsāraḥ syān na cāsti saḥ 
_
 nāsti cec chāśvataḥ kaścit ko bhaviṣyaty aśāśvataḥ 
_
 śāśvato 'śāśvataś cāpi dvābhyām ābhyāṃ tiraskṛtaḥ 
_

##
antavān yadi lokaḥ syāt paralokaḥ kathaṃ bhavet 
_
 athāpy anantavāl lokaḥ paralokaḥ kathaṃ bhavet 
_
 skandhānām eṣa saṃtāno yasmād dīpārciṣām iva 
_
 tasmān nānantavattvaṃ ca nāntavattvaṃ ca yujyate 
_
 pūrve yadi ca bhajyerann utpadyeran na cāpy amī 
_
 skandhāḥ skandhān pratītyemān atha loko 'ntavān bhavet 
_

##
pūrve yadi na bhajyerann utpadyeran na cāpyamī 
_
 skandhāḥ skandhān pratītyemāl loko 'nanto bhaved atha 
_
 antavān ekadeśaś ced ekadeśas tv anantavān 
_
 syād antavān anantaś ca lokas tac ca na yujyate 
_
 kathaṃ tāvad upādātur ekadeśo vinaṅkṣyate 
_
 na naṅkṣyate caikadeśa evaṃ caitan na yujyate 
_
 upādānaikadeśaś ca kathaṃ nāma vinaṅkṣyate 
_
 na naṅkṣyate caikadeśo naitad apy upapadyate 
_
 antavac cāpy anantaṃ ca prasiddham ubhayaṃ yadi 
_
 siddhe naivāntavat kāmaṃ naivānantavad ity api 
_
 atha vā sarvabhāvānāṃ śūnyatvāc chāśvatādayaḥ 
_
 kva kasya katamāḥ kasmāt saṃbhaviṣyanti dṛṣṭayaḥ 
_
 sarvadṛṣṭiprahāṇāya yaḥ saddharmam adeśayat 
_
 anukampām upādāya taṃ namasyāmi gautamam 
_
