 tādṛśakṛteḥ kārtrākhyātasthale dhātvarthaviśeṣyatayaiva bhānāt 
_
 evaṃ yājanatvasya yājanādikaraṇakatvasya pratyekameva vidheyatayā pratyekaṃ nāniṣiddhabrāhmaṇadhanārjanatvavyāpakatā 
_
 parasparaṃ vyabhicārāt 
_
 anyatamakaraṇakatvatvena vyāpakatāsattve 'pi noktavākyāt tallābhasambhavaḥ 
_
 tādṛśarūpasya vidheyatānavacchedakatvāt 
_
 vidheyatāvacchedakarūpeṇaiva ca vidheye vyāpakatābodhasya vyutpattisiddhatvāt 
_
 anyathātiprasaṅgāt 
_
 yājanāditritvasya tritayasādhāraṇatvena tattatkaraṇakatvatvena vyāpakatvasambhavāt 
_
 anyatamatvāghaṭitena tena rūpeṇa vyāpakatā vivakṣaṇīyetyapi na 
_
 tritvasya vyāsajyavṛttitayā tatparyāptyadhikaraṇakaraṇakatvasya pratyekamasattvenāprasiddhayā vyāpakatvāsambhavāt 
_
 paryāptisambandhenaiva ca bahuvacanasya tritvādyanvayabodhakatāvyutpatteḥ 
_
 paryāptyaghaṭitatadāśrayakaraṇakatvatvasya vidheyatāvacchedakatayā bhānāsambhavāt 
_
 evaṃ yājanāditritayaviśeṣitatritvasya na bahuvacanena prakṛtyarthaviśeṣyatayā bodhanasambhavaḥ bodho na sambhavati 
_
 yājanatvādyavacchinnaviśeṣaṇatayā bahuvacanārthatritvānvayāt 
_
 tathā ca tritvavad yājanatvādikameva vidheyatāvacchedakaghaṭakaṃ 
_
 tādṛśañca pratyekavṛttitayā na vyāpakatāvacchedakamiti na pratīkāraḥ 
_
yadi ca brāhmaṇo dhanamarjayedityato brāhmaṇakartṛkāniṣiddhadhanārjana tvāderuddeśyatāvacchedakatayā
abhāne 'pi tadrūpanirūpitavyāpakatā sambandhamaryādayā bhāsate ityupeyate
_
 upeyate ca vyutpattivaicitryād bahutvasya paryāptiṃ parityajyāśrayatāmātraṃ sambandhaḥ 
_
tadā pañca pañcanakhān bhuñjītetyādāvapi viparītaviśeṣyaviśeṣaṇabhāvalabhyasya
bhakṣyapañcanakhatvasya vākyāduddeśyatāvacchedakatayā abhāne 'pyetādṛśasamabhivyāhārasthale
tadrūpanirūpitāyāḥ śaśakādipañcakatvāśrayābhedatvāvacchinnavyāpakatāyāḥ
saṃsargamaryādayā bhānasambhavenetaranivṛttisiddhestatrāpi parisaṃkhyā na syāt
_
 idaṃ tu tattvam 
_
 uddeśyatāvacchedaka vidheyayorvyāpyavyāpakabhāvabodhanenānyathā vā yatretaranivṛttau viśrāmyanti tadaiva parisaṃkhyāvākyam 
_
 sa ca kvacit pravartakatayā parisaṃkhyāvidhiḥ imāmagṛbhṇannityādiḥ 
_
 kvacinniṣedhavannivṛttimātraparatayā avidhirūpaṃ parisaṃkhyāvākyaṃ pañca pañcanakhānityādi 
_
 yājanādhyāpanetyādikamapi parisaṃkhyaiveti

##
athaivaṃ sati parisaṃkhyāsthale svārthahānyādidoṣapravādasyāsaṅgatiḥ svārthaparityāgamantareṇāpi
parisaṃkhyāvidhisthale pañcanakhādiparisaṃkhyāsthale 'pi itaranivṛttisiddherdarśitatvāt
_
 evamṛtau bhāryāmupeyādityādiniyamo na tu parisaṃkhyā doṣatrayagrāsāditi sarvasiddhasiddhānto 'pi vyāhanyeta 
_
svārthahānyādimantareṇaivāniṣṭhānanubandhibhāryābhigamane ṛtukālavyāptibodhāt
parisaṃkhyāphalasyetarakāle bhāryābhigamananivṛttinirvāhānniyamānāvaśyakatvāt

##
na ca ṛtāvanabhigamane pratyavāyaśruteḥ ṛtukālābhigamanasyāvaśyakatve tattātparyamāvaśyakamiti tatra niyama iti vācyam 
_
anṛtāvabhigamanasyāpīndriyārtheṣu sarveṣu na prasajyeta kāmata ityādiniṣedhāt
tadekavākyatayā parisaṃkhyāyā api aucityāditi cenna
_
yena rūpeṇoddeśyatā tadanyarūpavyāpakatā uddeśya vidheyabhāvamaryādāto na labhyate iti pañca pañcanakhān
bhuñjītetyādāvuddeśyāviśeṣaṇa bhakṣyapañcanakhatvādau śaśakādyabhedavyāptyalābha ityāśayena tatra parisaṃkhyāyāṃ
svārthahānyādidoṣasya prācīnairabhidhānāt
_
ata eva śiromaṇibhaṭṭācāryairevakārārthavicāre iha bhavane maitreṇaiva pakṣyate temanamityatra
temanakarmakapākatvena pākānupasthityā tadrūpasyoddeśyatāvacchedakatvāsambhavāt
_
etadbhavanavṛttitvaviśeṣitatadrūpāvacchedena maitrānyakartṛkatvavyavacchedabodhasambhavāt
maitrānyasminnetadbhavanādhikaraṇakatemanakarmakapāka kartṛtvavyavacchedabodhāya evakārasya lakṣaṇā kṛtā
_
 prakṛte ca lakṣaṇopagame svārthaparityāgo dhruvaśca 
_
ṛtau svadārān gacchedityādau yadyapi liṅarthabalavadaniṣṭānanubandhitvaviśeṣita bhāryābhigamanatvānābhigamatvasya bhānāt
tadrūpasya kālaviśeṣasambandhoddeśyatāvacchedakatvaṃ sambhavatīti tadrūpe ṛtukālasambandhavyāptilābhaḥ sambhavati
_
tathāpi pacati caitro gacchatītyādyaprayogādākhyātārthasya vidheyatayaiva bhānaṃ
na tūddeśyatāvacchedakatayeti vyutpatterbalavadaniṣṭānanubandhitvarūpaliṅarthasya noddeśyatāvacchedakatvaṃ
vyutpattisiddhamityālocya tatra parisaṃkhyā nādṛtā
_
 kin tu ṛtukāle 'bhigamaniyama eva 
_
atha ṛtāviti vākyāt tatkāle 'bhigamananiyamabodhano 'pi tatkālīnānabhigamane 'niṣṭasādhanatvābodhakatayā tādṛśavidhivākyāt
vākyaṃ tatkālābhigamane pravṛttiniyamaṃ nopapādayatīti tasya niyamavidhitvaṃ durghaṭamiti cenna
_
tādṛśavākyasya tatkālīnābhigamane narakābhāvarūpeṣṭasādhanatābodhaka
tvopagamanottarakālamarthatastatkāla pratyāsannasyābhigamanābhāvasya narakasādhanatvabodhāt
tatkālīnābhigame niyatapravṛttyupapattyā tadvākyasya niyamavidhitvopapatteḥ
_
sandhyāmupāsītetyādinityavidhīnāṃ
kriyāsu narakābhāvasādhanatābodhakatayā uktarītyā tatra niyamapravṛttisampādakatve 'pi na teṣāṃ niyamavidhitvaṃ
_
 vidhiṃ vināpi yādṛśakriyāyāṃ rāgāditaḥ pravṛttisambhavastatra pravṛttiniyamopapādakavidhereva niyamavidhitvāt 
_
 paran tu sandhyāvandanādāvanyataḥ pravṛttyasambhavāt 
_
 tadvidhāyakavākyānāmapūrvavidhitvameva 
_

##
athoktarītyā ṛtāvityādividheḥ kriyāyāṃ niyatapravṛttinirvāhakatve tasya niyamabodhakatvaṃ
kimarthamupeyata iti cennopeyata eva tathātvaṃ niyamābodhakatve 'pyavaghātavidhivanniyamavidhitvasambhavāt
_
ṛtau bhāryāmupeyādevetyādivākye tvevakārasyāyogavyavacchedārthakatayā bhāryābhigame
ṛtukālavṛttyabhāvapratiyogitvavyavacchedarūpa vyāpakatābodhasyāvaśyakatayā tatraiva narakābhāvarūpeṣṭasādhanatānvayaḥ

##
kārtikādau haviṣyaṃ bhuñjītetyādirnāpūrvavidhiḥ 
_
 dugdhādirūpahaviṣyabhojane rāgata eva pravṛttisambhavāt 
_
 nāpi ṛtukālābhigamanādividhivanniyamavidhirakaraṇe pratyavāyasyāprāmāṇikatvāt 
_
nāpi parisaṃkhyāvidhiḥ māmagṛbhṇannityādergardhabharasanāgrahaṇe etanmantrasya
saphalatvajñānavirodhitayā tatra tanmantrapāṭhāpravṛttiprayojakatāvat
prakṛtasya haviṣyetarabhojane lokikapramāṇasiddhaphalake saphalatvajñānāvighaṭanena pravṛttivirahaprayojakatāyā asambhavāt
_
api tu haviṣyaṃ brahmacāritvaṃ brahmacaryaṃ ca mahāpātakanāśanamityādyarthavādabodhitaphalārthinaṃ
haviṣyetarabhojanādyabhāve pravartayan ekādaśyāmupavasedivadabhāvavidhireva
_
 na ca tadvākyāditarabhojanādyabhāvabodhāsambhavānna tadvidhitvasambhava iti vācyam 
_
 tathāvidhavidherarthavādena kalpyatve haviṣyetaranna bhuñjītetyādyākārakasyaiva kalpanāt 
_
 pratyakṣatve pañca pañcanakhānityādāviva lakṣaṇayābhimatārthabodhakatayā upapādanīyatvāt 
_
 arthavāde 'pi haviṣyādipadasya taditarabhojanādyabhāve lākṣaṇikatvopagamāt 
_
 bhuñjīta pitṛsevitamityatra śrāddhaniveditajātīyetaradravya bhojananiṣedhaikavākyayatayā parisaṃkhyāmeva svīkurvanti 
_
vastutaḥ pradakṣiṇamupavrajya bhuñjīta pitṛsevitamityādivacanairitikartavyatāsandaṃśena śeṣabhojanavidhānāt
śeṣabhojanasyāṅgatvaṃ
durapavādamiti śrāddhopakārakatayā tadvidhānena viniyogavidhiprabhedo niyatapravṛttiphalakatvānniyamavidhirūpo 'yam
_
śeṣasattvenāsyāṅgateti āvaśyakopavāsādidine śeṣabhojanamantareṇāpi pradhānanirvāhastatra
śrāddhe niḥśeṣānnamāṃsādidānena śrāddhaśeṣbhojanarūpāṅgasyānapekṣānirvāhāt
_
 evameva śrāddhavivekakṛtaḥ 
_
 na ca śrāddhajanyaparamāpūrve śeṣabhojanasya kāraṇatve kathaṃ tadaghaṭitasāmagrītaḥ paramāpūrvotpattiḥ 
_
 kāraṇatvābhāve śeṣasattve 'pi tadapekṣā na syāt kathaṃ vā tadaṅgaṃ bhavet 
_
sāvaśeṣaśrāddhajanyaparamāpūrve tasya kāraṇatvopagame 'pi
niravaśeṣaśrāddhajanyaparamāpūrvasāmagryā śeṣasattve 'pi tadbhojanamantareṇāpi akṣatatvāt
pradhānānirvāho durghaṭa itivācyam
_
śeṣabhojanavat śeṣābhāvasyāpi vaikalpikāṅgatvena śrāddhajanyaparamāpūrvasāmānyotpattau
śeṣābhāva śeṣabhojanarūpāṅgadvayānyatarasāmagryapekṣayā sāmañjasyāt
_
 na ca śeṣābhāvasyāṅgatābodhakavidhyabhāvāt kathaṃ tasyāṅgateti vācyam 
_
śeṣasattve kiṃ kurvītetyākāṅkṣyā pravṛtkasya śeṣānnaṃ
bhuñjītetividheḥ śeṣasattve bhojanāpekṣābodhakatayā śeṣāsattve tadanapekṣābodhakatayā śeṣāsattve śeṣabhojanaṃ
vinaiva pradhānasiddhirityetallābhāt
_
tatra śeṣābhāvasyānapekṣāyāṃ
śeṣasattve 'pi śeṣabhojanamantareṇāpūrvasya durvāratayā śeṣabhojanavidhestatra niyamena pravartakatvāyogād
vidhivaiyarthyena śeṣābhāvasyāṅgatāyāstadbodhaka
vidheścākṣepādubhayasthalasādhāraṇaḥ śeṣabhojanābhāvaviśiṣṭasya śeṣasyābhāva evāṅgam
_
bhuñjīta pitṛsevitamityanena viśeṣaṇābhāvarūpasya śeṣabhojanasya vastugatyā viśiṣṭābhāvatvāvacchinnaṃ
yadapūrvasādhanatvaṃ tadeva bodhyate
_
 tadrūpasya janakatāvacchedakañca vyabhicārāt 
_
 śeṣabhojanatvasya tathātvabādhāt taduttarakālakalpyaṃ 
_
 tāvatā viśeṣyābhāvasyāpi sādhāraṇarūpeṇāṅgatāsiddhirityapyāhuḥ
